Kingdom of Germaneigh: Warships (Konigliche Marine des Deutschland)



Germaneigh's navy isn't as impressive as the Ambleonian or Prench navies, and while they had a strong naval tradition, they never had nearly as many vessels as either nation. The Germane Fleet prided itself on strict discipline and dedication, and were usually regarded as to-a-pony more skilled at shipkeeping than the Ambleonian and Prench forces. Their warships rely on extremely heavy armor to last longer in a fight, and make use of hydraulics and steam power to reduce crewing requirements.
Germaneigh's ship prefix is "SMS" - or Seiner Majestat Schiff. They use a hull classification system more like Equestria's.

OVERALL LENGTH: 140 meters/459 feet NUMBER OF DECKS: 5 Belowdeck, 1 Casemate Deck, 1 Superstructure, 3 Conning Tower BEAM: 30 meters/98.425 feet DRAFT: 7.7 meters/25 feet
TRANSIT RANGE: 1710 nmi TOTAL CREW: 690 Enlisted, 100 Petty Officers, 40 Officers MARINE DETACHMENT: 120 NUMBER OF BOATS: 2 x 5 meter launches, 2 x 10 meter launch
The fat tubs that the Germane battelships are, they're incredibly steady gun platforms, even if their primary armament is quite light by comparison. They have advanced fire control capabilities, as well, making them INCREDIBLY accurate for naval guns. So, with a fast reload speed and high accuracy, Germane Battleships are liek fast firing snipers.

The Bomst and Thuringria classes are very visually similar, and very close in gun armament, making identification by enemy forces harder. The gun accuracy is still high, as with the Thuringrias, and doctrinally, they support the Battleships.

Just as fat, the hefty pork knuckles of the Hamover-class are faster than the larger vessels of the fleet, but about the slowest "Scout Cruisers" in NEIGHTO. Germaneigh doctrinally intends these vessels be used to support shore landings, and they have a higher Marine Force compliment than the larger vessels of the fleet, and more boats.

SHIP TYPE: Fleet Destroyer ("Zerstorer") ENGINES: 9 Bloom & Voss Triple Drum Boilers, 3 Porsche Geared Turbines SCREWS: 3 SPEED: 32kts

OVERALL LENGTH: 95 meters/328 feet NUMBER OF DECKS: 3/4 Belowdeck, 1 Superstructure + 2 Conning Tower BEAM: 12 meters/40 feet DRAFT: 4.1 meters/13.45 feet

The zippy little Zerstorers use conventional engines instead of Fractal Petroleum (Diesel) engines seen on most other smaller vessels in the NEIGHTO combined fleet. The Oxygen Torpedo used by the Germane Navy has a 20% higher top speed than its nearest peer, and comparable mechanical precision, meaning that German torpedo shots are faster and end up being more accurate as less probability of error exists that the target might move from the plotted course.

PRIMARY ARMAMENT: 2 x 1 88mm L/56 SECONDARY ARMAMENT: None TERTIARY ARMAMENT: 2 x 4, 2 x 1 500mm T3 Oxygen Torpedoes MACHINE GUNS: 5 x 2 11mm MG-3
OVERALL LENGTH: 60 meters/196 feet NUMBER OF DECKS: 2 Belowdeck + 1 Bridge BEAM: 9 meters/29.5 feet DRAFT: 3.5 meters/11.5 feet
TRANSIT RANGE: 500 nmi TOTAL CREW: 30 Enlisted, 10 Petty Officers, 5 Officers MARINE DETACHMENT: None NUMBER OF BOATS: None
The Schnellboots of the Germane navy are quite fast compared to the rest of their fleet, and represent the closest adaptation of and "armed racing schooner" of the three major European navies. As such, it has a very light gun armament, and relies on its torpedo compliment to inflict damage. Nimble enough to "chase shell splashes," these Germane naval hot rods can, in an emergency, go to 125% engine output and exceed 41 knots in a short run.
