Chapter 3: Greater Heights

Part 1: Pit March

TIME: 16:12 NERST
DATE: Redemption Day 6/12 Septimus, 2054 C.S.
LOCATION: North of Great Mareington, Maresechusetts, New Equestria


It was getting towards late afternoon. I was tired. I sat down on a rock, and motioned to everyone to stop.

Zipp: (To Hitch) "And he was the great hero?" She muttered.

Sunny: "I can hear you." She flicked an eye at Zipp.

K: "Zipp, I'm 44 years old. I spent the last ten years taking care of my dying wife. I didn't fight or train or anything in that time. Moreover, I wasn't in-shape until I got here. When I went back, I lost all my gains." I shrugged. "I'm past my prime in human years, and it shows. It's going to take me months to get back in any kind of shape."

Zipp: "Oh." She shrugged. "Sorry."

Izzy: "He just needs time." She bounced a bit. "We can stop early tonight!" She paced over into the woods, and came back a short time later, with all kinds of different things. "Mister Rock needs something." She smiled.

Hitch: "Anyway.. yeah." He pointed to a small cabin that was abandoned, but in decent shape. "That's a good spot to stop."

K: "Yeeep." I groaned as I got up, and downed the rest of my canteen. "Come on." I gestured.

Zipp: "Sorry.. K... just.. I'm used to high speed, low drag, yanno?" She tilted her head, pacing up next to me. "And what little I -do- know about you, you were supposed to be the best."

K: "That was the past." I nodded. "I need to get back in shape and start a new future." I gestured.

Sunny: "You can do it, Grandpa." She nodded.

Izzy: "Your sparkle will shine - just wait." She nodded, as she got the door open.

A few forest critters cleared out as we entered.

I noticed Hitch follow them with his eyes, carefully. And shrugged, as Zipp stepped in and whipped out her cell phone.

Zipp: "Sixteen-thirty hours, 12th of Septimus." She started. "Bivouacking early. Cabin in the woods, north of Mareington." She looked around.

K: "What's all your training, Zipp?" I asked, casually.

Zipp: "Oh, I did a rotation with the AFSOC team." She shrugged. "And I've got my training and interest in investigations." She gestured.

K: "You seemed awfully interested in me appearing in Bridlewood." I said, sitting down.

Zipp: *T'chah* "Yeah. It's like.." She held her hooves up, looking at the paws of her mitts. "..no one cares about anything any more. They just go along to get along."

K: "You feel the magic of friendship might be.. losing something?" I leaned in a bit.

Zipp: "Yeah... maybe..." She looked sullen, all of a sudden.

K: "And, let me guess, your senior officers see you as a wild mustang that won't calm down, and just.. go along to get along?" I smirked, leaning in behind her head.

Zipp: "Yeah! Exactly! They never give me any credit!" She swept her hoof wide. "And when I have concerns? They never authorize me to do anything about it." She nodded. "Then, I do it, and they just act like it was all their idea or whatever." She snorted.

K: "Well, I'm the exact opposite. You seem to have good instincts. I trust you." I pat her on the shoulder. "If you need to investigate something, though, I only ask you tell me everything, without reservations or fears. I listen, and I listen good to the ponies that have skill and talent. That's why.." I stepped away to help build the fire. "..I want you in CAG."

Zipp: "WHAT?!" She looked at me, stunned. "REALLY?!"

K: "Yup." I nodded, helping build the fire.

Zipp: "Oh, zip-eriffic!" She smiled, broadly.

Sunny: "Friends?" She held out her hoof.

Zipp: "Friends!" She shook, but Izzy dove in and hugged them both.

So, Sunny, Izzy, Zipp, and Hitch. My team was growing. We settled in for the evening, and drew shifts. Sunny made sure I was taking salt tablets, to keep my electrolytes up, and made sure I had an electrolyte powder drink extra. Dinner was ration oats, a large tin of beans we found in one of the cupboards that was nearing but not at its expiry date, and some hardtack soaked into the beans.

The others actually fell asleep on me, leaving me with Zipp.

Zipp: "Is it bad that I want to see you chew out my CO?" She smirked.

K: "Nah, not at all." I shrugged. "Who are they?"

Zipp: "Major Parsley." She nodded.

K: "Oh, you dropped this." I pulled out her ID card, and handed it to her.

Zipp: "Huh!" She smirked. "Funny that I'm the one looking for clues all the time, and I left one behind like that." She shrugged her wings.

K: "I hope I can find out what direction I need to go on. Strategically speaking." I nodded.

Zipp: "I think you can." She smiled a bit. "At least I know, you'll never blow me off."

K: "Never." I grinned.


TIME: 23:14 NERST


We heard something moving outside. Something big. Something kinda scary sounding. It was Hitch's watch, and he just poked all of us to get us up. We all, quietly, got to our weapons. I opened one of the cabin windows, closed with shutters, and I was greeted by the sight of something that looked almost..

K: "GIZMONK!" I aimed at it, my eyes identifying a weapon in its hands, thumb flicked the Manticore to hyperburst, and I fired. The recoil almost pushed me over, I wasn't ready for it.

Hitch: "WHAT'S THAT?!" He opened the window by him, and two of them dove through, trying to grab at him.

Sunny: "GET OFF HIM!" She charged forwards, and mule kicked, sending one into a wall, and the other back out the window.

Zipp got up next to me, and pulled her sidearm, quickly mounting the suppressor, and starting to fire shots at anything moving. Izzy moved to the windows facing the back, and started shooting her Manticore first on single shot, then seeing as that was less than effective, switched to full auto - hers was set around 600 rounds a minute, and that was quite controllable.

One Hitch got back up, he fired a Flare grenade out of the window at an upwards angle. The area was illuminated, and I could see these "Gizmonks" now - whatever they were, they were solidly black, with glowing yellow eyes, but wearing clothes and carrying rifles. They fired back intermittently, and we returned fire smartly, now that we could see them.

When they went down, they stayed down.

It was over quickly, with several of them breaking and running for the treeline. Before I could stop her, Zipp got a running start, and threw herself out of the window, catching some air for a moment, and firing down at them rapidly, expending a full magazine before landing, and killing the last four before they could make good their escape.

Attacked in the middle of the night by a Platoon-sized force. By something. Some one. I had no idea what or who, but I knew my enemy when I saw them.

That made my return even more dangerous. For everyone.

[End of Part 1]


Part 2: Firelight

TIME: 00:07 NERST
DATE: Redemption Day 7/13 Septimus, 2054 C.S.
LOCATION: North of Great Mareington, Maresechusetts, New Equestria


The attack had been sudden, but I got to see that my new team had it together. They could fight, and they could kill, without having to be coached into it. I had a feeling, that like ponies were, they would do whatever was needed to protect their friends. Even if it meant taking lives.

Also, it looked that, despite the lack of magic, Zipp could fly? I knew for a fact that Pegasi flight was magical. And if Magic was sealed, this made no sense.

Once it was over, and Zipp came back in, Sunny was astounded.

Sunny: "YOU CAN FLY!" She pointed at her.

Zipp: "Aaaaaaaaahhhh.... not.. quite?" She tilted her head.

Hitch: "Then what do you call what you just did?" He gestured his foreleg at her.

Zipp: "F-Falling with.. style?" She rubbed her right fetlock on her right upper foreleg.

K: "You can glide." I nodded. "I'm guessing that's the closest any Pegasi has to actual flight?"

Zipp: "Y-Yeah.." She sighed and lowered her head. "Please don't tell.."

Sunny: "No, no, I get it, you're fine!" She hugged her. "Still, that's so awesome!"

Izzy: "I guess it's like me being able to see everyone's sparkle!" She chimed in, and slid between the other two mares. "Like, I can tell all kinds of things by looking at your Sparkle. You gotta let it shiiiiine!" She waved her foreleg in front of her, slowly.

K: "..we have a problem." I said, having finished examining the corpse. "These things are real." I turned, looking at them.

Hitch: "Well, I mean, it looks real enough to me." He shrugged.

Zipp: "Nooo, Hitch. K means these are living creatures." She moved over and examined the corpse herself. "And they bleed red blood."

K: "Whatever it is that's causing this blackness? I don't know." I shrugged. "But.. something is controlling these creatures." I gestured at the dead body. "This is a Gizmonk. Yes, it's slightly mutated from what I recall, but.. it's still a Gizmonk. A lot of them worked for Tirek a thousand years ago." I sighed.

Izzy: "You don't think Tirek's back, do you?" She tilted her head.

K: "No. This is something else." I dragged the corpse over into the light better. "But, whatever it is, it knows I'm here, now. And I have a feeling, these were sent to stop me. Stop us. From doing whatever it is we need to do." I shrugged. "Alright, let's police the bodies."

I went over and picked up the rifle it had been carrying. It was strange, it looked like a Bradley. It took me a bit to remember how to take it apart, but I did. And what I found, shocked me even more.

Hitch: "What's up, K?" He leaned over. "Uh - SUNNY!"

Sunny: "What's up, Hitch?" She leaned in. "Oh.. no."

There on the bolt was a proof stamp from the Appleloosa Arsenal. In Texas. I held it up, and showed it off.

K: "Someone stole my factory." I frowned. "And they're using it."

Izzy: "Yeah." She poked the bolt with her hoof. "No chance this was copied or anything." She shrugged.

I explained that the Appleloosa Arsenal was the first major weapons factory that we had. And what we went through to get it.

Zipp: "Zero-zero-seven hours, 13th. Attacked by platoon sized formation of enemies - K calls them Gizmonks. They were armed with one thousand year old assault rifles, apparently made at Appleloosa Arsenal in the lost land of Texas." She said, into her cell phone.

K: "Good. Take notes. We might need them later." I nodded to her.

Hitch: "I'll go police up the bodies." He nodded.

Izzy: "I'll help!" She bounced after him.

Sunny: "What does this mean?" She shrugged.

K: "I think.. like I said.. someone knows I'm here and they're trying to stop us." I shrugged.

Zipp: "It's bad." She looked at me. "Can I give you a briefing?"

K: "Give me everything." I nodded.

Zipp: "I've been following strange incidents for the last several months. Odd stuff. Stolen goods, missing ponies, sightings of creatures no one has seen before." She shrugged. "Most of it is in my office. I didn't know.. didn't think.. you might come back, or.. even if you were real, but.." She dismissively waved. "..I'm starting to think this is all related." She nodded. "After you meet the Prime Minister, I'll show you everything." She looked at Sunny. "I don't know the old legends, so, this is all new to me. I'm trying to come at this from an investigative perspective."

Sunny: "I know all about the old legends. I can teach you - and so can K. He IS most of those legends." She nodded vigorously.

K: "Well, we'll see what we can do." I shrugged. "Once we get to Pitfield, we'll see if we can get a riverboat to the north."

Zipp: "I took one from the Greenbrooke border to Pitfield, and I caught a ride with a Gendarmes unit to the Erie border." She looked at me. "That's how I caught up with you."

K: "Understood." I nodded. "I mean, we spent a few days in Bridlewood. Where were you when you found out about me?"

Zipp: "Pitfield. I overheard.. well.. spied.. on a radio report saying someone strange arrived in Bridlewood." She shrugged her wings. "So, I headed towards it and kinda met you part way."

K: "Makes sense. How long would a riverboat trip take to the Greenbrooke border?" I nodded to her as Hitch and Izzy came back in.

Zipp: "Less than a day." She smiled.

K: "Good." I nodded.

I wanted to meet the Prime Minister as soon as possible. This was getting serious, and security had to be on alert throughout whatever there was of the kingdom. If they attacked me here, they might attack elsewhere.

[End of Part 2]

TIME: 17:17 NERST
DATE: Redemption Day 7/13 Septimus, 2054 C.S.
LOCATION: Pitfield, Maresechusetts, New Equestria


Pitfield was a proper town. Once we entered, we were taken by "golf cart" to the Gendarmes Station. Sheriff Capped Lens was there to greet us. She looked exactly like a female Wide Lens. And she knew immediately who I was.

Cap: "GENERAL K!" She dove into my arms. "Oh, hi, Hitch." She said dismissively.

K: "GACK!" I was almost being strangled, she was quite strong.

Cap: "Call me Cap." She kissed my cheek. "OH my STARS and HOOVES, I'm so glad the legends were true." She pulled on me and showed me a magnifying glass, encased in a glass case. "This was grandma's." She pointed to it with her hoof. "It's been passed down in my family for generations - that you'd come. You'd save us."

Zipp: "Sheriff." She nodded.

K: "Well, at least there's some that'll know who I am." I shrugged.

Cap: "Yeah." She smiled, wistfully. "But, it also means trouble." She leaned over to the radio. C> "This is Golf-Alpha-One-One to all patrols. Yellow alert. Shift C report to your posts." And shrugged.

Hitch: "So, Cap.. incident reports?" He shrugged.

Cap: "Yeah, yeah, Captain Storm wanted to know if any strange and unsolvable crimes have been happening. So, I took a look, and sure enough, we've got 16 incidents that are very, very unusual." Hitch: "A cheese sandwich and carrots." He nodded.

Sunny: "Tomato soup if you have it. Or chicken." She smiled.

I want to do things. Investigate, be a detective, be an officer, do my duty, and.. help ponies." She snorted a sigh. Zipp: "PIPP!" She blinked. "Ah, I-Er.." She lowered her head. "..yeah, sorry I haven't been around much, lately.."

K: "Well, then, it's nice to meet you, Pipp." I said, announcing myself.

Pipp: "Wait.." She turned, looked, and JUST realized I was there. She went for her cell phone, but Zipp stopped her. "Ziiiiiiiiiiiiiipppp! THIS. IS. Ha-yoo-uuuge! I've GOT to-"

I could show Ponies that, together, we could do anything. I was at one hand elated, and at another, a little reserved. I'd have to keep a handle on her. Not that I wasn't used to it - Elsie Bon Voyage came to mind.

Still, we had a new friend. And Friendship is Magic.

Part 4: River Rats

TIME: 08:02 NERST
DATE: Redemption Day 8/14 Septimus, 2054 C.S.
LOCATION: Pitfield, Maresechusetts, New Equestria


We had access to a river boat, thanks to Sheriff Cap. The river boat was technically co-owned by the Gendarmes and the town itself. It transported people and goods from Zephyr Heights to points south, so it made regular trips down the Husatonic. Most trips were directly to Pitfield, and then once a week, down south further to Cobnecticut.

We got up early, so we could meet the river boat, and get on board before it needed to leave. Pipp held my arrival a secret, and did her morning podcast with her sister, though at my suggestion, she asked her "Pippsqueaks" to report any strange sightings. It then showed me that I could do this, too - appeal to the masses for help.

The river boat captain was very happy to have us aboard. The riverboat had a machine gun, but no other weapons. The machine gun was interesting - it bore a structural resemblance to the Abrams, but it was chambered for 6mm Caseless. It had a hopper on the side to fit standard Manticore magazines into. It made sense, you didn't want to put a belt link on caseless ammo. The hopper held 6 magazines, for 360 rounds on tap. The hopper could be refilled one at a time, if needed, or the whole hopper could be detached and the weapon reloaded by swapping full hoppers.

K: "Zipp." I pointed to the Machine Gun.

Zipp: "Note: Bigger machine gun." She looked at me, and tilted her head, like, did I get it right?"

I nodded to her in the affirmative, and smiled. She smiled, back, and we bro-hoof'd. We were meshing. Pipp was busy filming at the back of the boat, to keep her audience distracted.

Hitch: "I see you two are getting along good." He nodded, pacing up. "I could use a good detective in Maretime Bay!" He smiled at Zipp.

Zipp: "Oh? Got a problem finding your horseshoes?" She smirked at him, leaning in a bit, raising an eyebrow.

I didn't see it, but Sunny gave a half-glare to the interaction.

I leaned on the machine gun mount as the river boat pulled away from shore. I didn't have my sea legs.

K: "Ahh.. where's Izzy?" I looked around.

Sunny: "Huh?" She looked around. "Oh, no!" She trotted off. "I saw her get on the boat..!"

Zipp: "You know, I hear you're kinda popular." She said to Hitch.

Zipp: "Uh-huh. I might've gotten one of your calendars in the mail." She snickered.

Zipp: "Ah, it's chill." She patted his back.

Interesting.. I wasn't entirely sure if they were flirting, or just feeling each other out, but I noted that Zipp was very much a tomboy. Reminded me of J--, honestly, a bit, but not in the same way.

Sunny returned with Izzy in short order, and Izzy was using her Titan's Mitts and walk boots so she could assemble something with her forelimbs while walking. She put together a fascinating decoration. I noted it had glitter in it. She put it on the superstructure near the main hatch to the inside.

Fascinating.

Part 5: At the Heights

TIME: 15:21 NERST
DATE: Redemption Day 9/15 Septimus, 2054 C.S.
LOCATION: Zephyr Heights, Greenbrooke, New Equestria


The offices of the national government were partly located here, as Zipp explained to me, and as most of the city was populated by Pegasi, this is where Haven preferred to stay. Her personal offices were here.

Zipp called her mom before we arrived, and told her she wanted to see her. It'd been a time since she'd seen her mom, so, Haven - I was warned - was going to be very excited to see her and Pipp together.

As we entered the government offices, I was struck by how much it resembled Canterlot. Like, they'd lost Canterlot, and copied a lot of its trappings here. It was... bittersweet at best. I didn't pay that much attention to all of what Zipp was saying as she led us through the building. It functioned as a substitute Royal Palace, Parliament, and where Theia and Haven primarily lived. We finally got to the throne room, where Theia usually held court - and there was Haven.

Zipp: "..and this is my mother, General." She nodded.
