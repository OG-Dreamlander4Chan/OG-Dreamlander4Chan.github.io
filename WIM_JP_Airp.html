Empire of Neighpon: Aircraft (Teikoku Kūgun)



The Imperial Air Force was raised from nothing, trained and learned through painful lessons, and trial-and-error. Aircraft designs were many, all workable ideas tried, and a large number of prototypes created. From this, a number of very good designs were created, and some unique features incorporated, including expanded use of Scandium Alloy. Scandium, when alloyed properly, has the strength of titanium, the light weight of aluminum, and the hardness of ceramic. Extensive use of it in most Neighponese aircraft, allow them to withstand greater stresses than other NEIGHTO aircraft, and maintain neutral buoyancy with smaller gas bag diameters.


S1A "HAJIME"
DESIGNATION: Nacowjima S1A "Hajime" ROLE: Scout/Light Anti-Sub/Light Utility Aircraft AIRCRAFT TYPE: Lifting Body with neutral buoyancy
GUN ARMAMENT: 1 x 1 7mm Nagoya Type-03A GPMG ORDNANCE ARMAMENT: Up to 250lbs of explosives HARD POINTS: 1 under-fuselage
OVERALL LENGTH: 6.88 meters/22.5 feet OVERALL HEIGHT: 2.36 meters/7.74 feet (Landing Gear retracted) ENGINES: 1 Suzuki VM-02 Fractal Petroleum
CRUISE SPEED: 60kts/111kph/69mph MAXIMUM SPEED: 87kts/161kph/100mph FERRY RANGE: 720 nmi
This is the primary scout plane of the Japanese fleet. With a center-mounted 7mm Machine Gun, the Neighponese had to invent an interruptor gear to allow the weapon to fire through the single propeller. Like all NEIGHTO aircraft, it's a lifting body and neutral buoyancy design. It's primary intent is reconnaissance, and anti-submarine duty. Armament is one single depth charge under the main body. They're supposed to drop in a formation for the best chance of a hit. The hardpoint can also accept a two-bomb rack of 45kg/100lb fragmentation bombs for light troop support. The only down side is that both bombs get released at the same time. The craft can have up to two ponies inside of it, side-by-side, for personnel transfers.
