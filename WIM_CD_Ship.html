Federal Republic of Canida: Warships (Canidan Forces Navy)



The only Representative Government in North Equus, Canida is much smaller than its Earth counterpart of Canada. While it has (most) of the same Provinces, and the same relative population, its territory is, relatively, to the west of its Continential Partner counterpart, Equestria vs. United States. As such, it doesn't have access to the "east coast" of Equestria, and its shipyards are located along what would be the Pacific Coast. Thus, its Navy is much smaller than other NEIGHTO partners. Canida, however, chose to focus on quality as much as possible, over quantity. Its ships are very potent units, and pre-planned for the likeihood that Tirek would deploy powered aircraft.
Canida's major naval base is Barkover (Vancouver), but they have shipyards at Sandwich (Saanich/Victoria), Allford Bay on Graham Island, and the city of Mount Wolfenden. Barkover, however, contains the nation's main dry docks and construction yards that can handle the assembly of large steel hulled vessels.
Canida uses the prefix "RCS" (Repubic of Canida Ship) for its vessels. It also uses Equestria's Hull Classification system.

The largest and heaviest vessels that Canida can construct, when faced with a choice between trying to craft a Battlecruiser without the ability to produce heavy cannon, or an aircraft carrier, Canidan planners went with the Carrier option. Plans for HMS Cloudsdale were shared, and Canidan ship planners took the opposite tack - placing the superstructure on the forward quarter of the ship instead of the rear, for better maneuverability at the cost of more precise control of flight operations. The CFA-1 Firefox is just as unique as the ship that carries it, and the CS-1 Hound more a multirole aircraft than just a simple scout.
