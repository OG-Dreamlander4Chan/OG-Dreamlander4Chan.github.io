My Little Pony: Warfare is Magic

Empire of Neighpon: Firearms



While Neighpon received a great deal of War Aid from Equestria, the moment his nation was freed, Emperor no Ken demanded his industrial facilities begin producing their own arms as soon as possible. The easiest thing to do was to produce more Bradley rifles, but with locally produced furniture, at first. Later on, more weapons of indigenous design were made. Neighpon transformed several major industrial companies over to producing weapons and ammo.



Type 33 "Yumi"
WEAPON TYPE: Double Action Semi-Automatic Pistol
MANUFACTURER: Shin-Chuō/Nambu Industries
CALIBER: 9x23mm NEIGHTO
CAPACITY: 10 + 1 Round Detachable Box Magazine
EFFECTIVE RANGE: 50 meters
BARREL LENGTH: 120mm/4.75 inches
OVERALL LENGTH: 224mm/8.81 inches
OVERALL HEIGHT: 150mm/5.9 inches
TOTAL MASS: 1.77lbs/0.8kg
The Type 33 Yumi is a fairly standard pistol in the NEIGHTO alliance, with a 10 + 1 round capacity, and good balance. The slide release is a button that's easily poked by a hoof tip, but only activates when the slide is pulled ALL the way to the rear, so the chamber can be inspected, or the weapon disassembled.
Type 34 "Tenryu"
WEAPON TYPE: Personal Defense Weapon
MANUFACTURER: Mineba Mitsumi Company
CALIBER: 9x23mm NEIGHTO
CAPACITY: 30 Round Detachable Box Magazine
EFFECTIVE RANGE: 120 meters
BARREL LENGTH: 192mm/7.55 inches
OVERALL LENGTH: 600mm/23.6 inches (stock unfolded)
346mm/13.6 inches (stock folded)
OVERALL HEIGHT: 226mm/8.9 inches
TOTAL MASS: 5.8lbs/2.63kg
Developing the telescoping bolt, Neighpon was able to make their SMG into a PDW. Every billet that would require both an SMG and a Pistol, is issued only this instead, and more ammunition. The handiness of a weapon this small when folded, that can still be fired this way, can't be understated. Requiring an extra large holster, most Ponies can fit this along the side of their bodies, or in a flank holster at an angle. The holster itself automatically deploys the foregrip when the weapon is drawn, too, allowing an immediate two-hoof/hand grip. A Human/Canidan version was presented to General K.
Type 33 "Shugekiryu"
WEAPON TYPE: Assault Rifle
MANUFACTURER: Mitsubishi Heavy Industries
CALIBER: 7x40mm NEIGHTO
CAPACITY: 30 Round Detachable Box Magazine
EFFECTIVE RANGE: 400 meters
BARREL LENGTH: 360mm/14.2 inches
OVERALL LENGTH: 790mm/31.1 inches (stock unfolded)
556mm/21.8 inches (stock folded)
OVERALL HEIGHT: 242mm/9.52 inches
TOTAL MASS: 6.95lbs/3.15kg
A standard Bradley, but with a folding stock instead of a collapseable one. The adapter for the Type-34 "Ryu no Yari" (Dragon Lance) Grenade Launcher (a copy of the Gavin) is much more simple than the Bradley's adapter, and can be mounted and dismounted with the removal of the front pivot pin, installation of the adapter rail (with launcher), and replacement of the pivot pin with the Gremade Launcher's pin. There's even a slot for the original pivot pin, in a small compartment in the stock.
