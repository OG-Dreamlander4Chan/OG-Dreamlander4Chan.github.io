My Little Pony: Marking Warfare's Tale

Kingdom of New Equestria: Vehicles



New Equestria's military is limited in its production capacity, and as such, focuses on a small number of highly capable vehicles. Soon after General K's reappearance, petroleum distillate production - long a problem - was greatly increased, as was a move towards as many alternative fuel sources as possible, especially solar panels. Electronics equipment varies by region and town, with some units afforded the newest and best, and the constabulary troops largely doing with the basic necessities. The 'newest and best,' however, is extremely high technology stuff. Most of the electronics equipment is produced by Canterlogic Industries.


Joint Light Tactical Vehicle (Scout)
DESIGNATION: M118A2 'Ghost' JLTV Recon/Scout ROLE: Light Scout and Recon Vehicle, Mine Protected ARMOR: 12.5mm (Cab Body), 25mm (Cab Underside)
MAIN GUN: None MACHINE GUN: 1 Turret Weapon Interface Mount AMMUNITION: None
OVERALL LENGTH: 4.88 meters/16 feet OVERALL HEIGHT: 2.24 meters/7.35 feet ENGINES: 1 Gale Clouds Engineering 86T V-10, 450hp
POWER TO WEIGHT RATIO: 48kw/tonne | 64.4hp/t MAXIMUM SPEED: 115kph/71mph (On Road) TRAVEL RANGE: 320 km
New Equestria's rapid response vehicle, the JLTV-RS carries up to seven (eight in cramped conditions) passengers, has a very high top speed, and has armor plate capable of withstanding 6mm AP Caseless at ranges over 12 meters. The windows are reinforced to stop 6mm Caseless Ball at point-blank range. Standard configuration is for a Scout Team to operate two vehicles with three ponies each, and carry extra cargo with the remaining interior space. Since Patrols can take up to two weeks in a circuit and cover some very heavy terrain, Recon-Scout Teams are equipped for as many eventualities as possible. All vehicles have winch packages capable of pulling up to 16,000lbs/7300kg. They also regularly carry various road tools, food, and medical supplies.
Joint Light Tactical Vehicle (Guardian)
DESIGNATION: M119A1 'Phantom' JLTV Air/Land Defense ROLE: AA or AT Missile Carrier, Mine Protected ARMOR: 12.5mm (Cab Body), 25mm (Cab Underside), 8mm (Turret)
MAIN GUN: LIM-9 Bugbear or LAM-4 Stoneback MACHINE GUN: None AMMUNITION: 1 Missile Reload carried
OVERALL LENGTH: 4.88 meters/16 feet OVERALL HEIGHT: 2.84 meters/9.3 feet ENGINES: 1 Gale Clouds Engineering 86T V-10, 450hp
POWER TO WEIGHT RATIO: 46kw/tonne | 61.6hp/t MAXIMUM SPEED: 103kph/64mph (On Road) TRAVEL RANGE: 320 km
The Phantom Guardian vehicle is an M119 Cargo Carrier with a Guardian missile pod attached to the back. The multi-function turret can launch either Bugbear SAM's or Stoneback ATGM's. Both of these are infantry-portable missile systems, and an emergency launcher adapter is carried in the vehile (most of the time) to allow the missiles to be used without the turret. The Phantom Guardian is geenrally deployed only at airfields and used by the New Equestrian Air Force, though a smaller number are employed by the Army as a doctrinal replacement for an anti-tank or anti-air vehicle where possible. The base M119 Cargo Carrier is essentially the same, but without a weapon pod on the back.
Light Armor Strike Vehicle
DESIGNATION: M126A1/A2/A3 'Knight' LASV ROLE: Mine Resistant, Ambush Protected Wheeled Armored Personnel Carrier ARMOR: 37mm (Hull sides, front, rear), 44mm (Hull bottom), 25mm (Hull top)
MAIN GUN: None MACHINE GUN: None AMMUNITION: None
OVERALL LENGTH: 7 meters/23 feet OVERALL HEIGHT: 2.5 meters/8.2 feet ENGINES: 1 Gloworm G32 I-8, 420hp
POWER TO WEIGHT RATIO: 19kw/tonne | 25.5hp/t MAXIMUM SPEED: 100kph/62mph (On Road) TRAVEL RANGE: 320 km
The Knight is the basis of a family of vehicles that fills out the middle rank of Equestria's vehicluar needs. Mixed steel and ceramic armor allows it to shrug off some serious punishment, while its powerful engine can get some serious speed when on flat terrain. The base APC version can hold a total of 12 occupants, plus basic field packs. The LASV's are mainly used in convoy protection, since their armor can withstand some lf the larger monsters that have been found in some places in New Equestria since the cataclysm. Standard loadout is a driver, navigator, and a six pony squad. It has the ability to attach an Infantry Weapons Interface Mount to the upper turret ring, to allow weapons to be used from a stable brace.
