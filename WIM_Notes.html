Chapter 0/Orignal Autor's Note:

This is the original Dream, as originally posted on 4chan /mlp/, and why I use the name Dreamlander. This is unaltered, except for fixing spelling errors.

Congrats, /mlp/, you sick bastards did this to me. I swear. The only times I come here is to get Pinkie Pie pics, for my wife's friend.. and started watching on her recommendation. Then, a few days ago, I had a nightmare of epic proportions.. mixed with some better parts.

Before I get to the horror that you gave me, I feel it's important to explain that I have a long history of especially realistic dreaming. To the point that I can taste, touch, smell, read, hold conversations, and see things I've never seen before. Take that as you will. Now, the dream.

OTHER IMPORTANT NOTES:

-The main character, "K," does not know what the Equestrian calendar is, until Chapter 38. While calendar dates are provided sparsely up until that point, be aware that the main character DOES NOT know what they are, until he's specifically told.
