Nations of the World: Rank Systems



The old NEIGHTO organization had a unified rank system, and over the last thousand years, things did change across the world, but the equivalence of rank systems somewhat held.


Kingdom of New Equestria

One thousand years ago, Equestria led the war effort against the Dark King, Tirek. The rank system consisted of pins affixed to collars, usually made of gold and silver, as the metals - while precious - could also be conjoured by Princesses Celestia and Luna. Princess Theia, daughter of Luna, who's Cutie Mark adorns the new national flag, inherited a shattered quarter of the kingdom after the Apocalypse. With magic gone, and resources few, rank insigna reverted to cloth for the lower ranks, and plated zinc for warrant officers and officers, with cloth stitching used to represent ranks on combat uniforms. The rank system was also somewhat simplified. With the general fracturing of the Kingdom, senior officer ranks soetimes went unfilled. Until the return of General K, nopony was granted a rank higher than Major General (OF-08), with a Lieutenat General (OF-09) serving as Theia's advisor. The rank of General (OF-10) was reserved for General K's possible return, and in the tradition of her mother and aunt, it is a pin bearing Theia's Cutie Mark.
In addition to the standard Other Ranks (OR), a series of Special Technical (ST) ranks were created, for those that have expert knowledge in some area, and require authority over work crews, but not authority in a combat situation.
