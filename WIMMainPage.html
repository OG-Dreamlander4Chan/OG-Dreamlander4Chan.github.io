My Little Pony: Warfare is Magic



Here it is - the main story. The whole reason I got going with this, in the first place, and started writing "FIMFiction." Now, because this is the main story, there's a LOT of stuff going on here - things I created to help people better visualize the world I'm writing. PICTURES! Lots of pictures. And details about them, as well. Extensive details. Why? Because, I was ORIGINALLY a poster on 4Chan's /k/ (Weapons) board. I came over to the /mlp/ board on a lark, one day, and started collecting pictures. Until the fateful day that THIS story came into my head.
