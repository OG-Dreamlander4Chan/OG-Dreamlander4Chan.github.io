AHR Macedonkia: Warships (Assinan Mornaritsa)



Macedonkia's navy was designed to be used almost wholly within the Agean Sea, which they have ports along. While the fleet is capable of high seas operations, they are notably less steady in rough seas than NEIGHTO vessels. When Macedonkia declared war on NEIGHTO along with most of the nations of the Non-Aligned Movement, they had a fleet roughly equal to Vulpesylvania. However, they lacked aircraft entirely. Almost all naval assistance came from the Herdiet Union in terms of design and layout, after their first generations of ships were launched. Most of these first generation vessels were kept around as coastal defense vessels, save their first Battleships, which were reclassified as Monitors.
Ship names listed here are only those known by NEIGHTO. Macedonkia uses a simple "yard construction number" system, but unlike Prance, paints their hulls with these numbers. Ships are numbered sequentially starting from the date they're laid down.

Part of the same Common Hull Design as the Heavy Cruisers and Light Cruisers, the thinner hull armor of the Kozjak class compared to NEIGHTO designs isn't as much of an impediment when one keeps in mind, it was originally only supposed to be proofed to the pre-refit Prince Orlando class battlecruisers. Part of the reason for the invasion in the first place was the fear that the upgraded Prince Orlandos were now an overmatch to these vessels. The unusal 13.8-inch guns are enough to overmatch the armor on the Prince Orlandos, but fall short of proper NEIGHTO Battleships.

TRANSIT RANGE: 470 nmi TOTAL CREW: 439 Enlisted, 41 Petty Officers, 25 Officers MARINE DETACHMENT: 24 NUMBER OF BOATS: 4 x 5 meter launches
These tiny ships were once the pride Battleships of the Macedonkian fleet. Not to leave them unused, the Macedonkians recomissioned them as Monitors for use close in-shore to bombardment targets, and as carriers for landing craft. While its normal Marine compliment is 24, it can embark up to 120, and up to 10 launches total, allowing it to land two companies of troops, effectively, and give them naval gun fire support. Though retasked for landing operations, these Monitors are capable of going toe-to-toe with Vulpesylvania's coastal defense battleships.

Macedonkian heavy cruisers are rather impressive for the type, with 15 guns, giving it a VERY impressive broadside. However, it is purely a Gun Cruiser, with no ability to use torpedoes, no scout aircraft, no anti-aircraft guns, and no anti-submarine protection. The strength of the main battery cannot be understated, however, and can present a hazard to even lighter armored battleships, and were intended to be able to hazard the Prince Orlando class Battlecruisers.

The tail end of the Common Hull Design, the Macedonkian Light Cruisers are a force to be reckoned with. While as vulnerable to aircraft and submarines as the rest of the fleet, they do sport two quintuple torpedo launchers, and a fairly strong gun for a light cruiser. As with the rest of the Common Hull Design ships, armor is very good, and superior to other ships of the type, stillusing Void-Fill-Void style sandwich construction, and concreted armor. This allows it to resist hits from shells up to 7-inch across the hull.
Danube Class

AIRCRAFT COMPLIMENT: None FLIGHT CREW: None ANTI-SUBMARINE ARMAMENT: None TORPEDO ARMAMENT: 2 x 5 550mm Steam Torpedo
OVERALL LENGTH: 90 meters/295.3 feet NUMBER OF DECKS: 3 Belowdeck, 1 Superstructure + 1 Bridge BEAM: 14 meters/45.9 feet DRAFT: 4.1 meters/13.45 feet
TRANSIT RANGE: 1000 nmi TOTAL CREW: 92 Enlisted, 26 Petty Officers, 12 Officers MARINE DETACHMENT: 24 NUMBER OF BOATS: 2 x 5 meter launches
Macedonkian destroyers, like the rest of the fleet, are very similar to design concepts of Earth's 1920's to 1930's period. Lacking Anti-Aircraft guns and Anti-Sumbarine weapons like the rest of the fleet, the Destroyers are purely attack vessels. They were outfitted with early Sonar, however. Macedonkia's destroyers were very numerous, suggesting they put a lot of faith in their gun power and torpedo ability - which is easily on par with any NEIGHTO destroyer.
