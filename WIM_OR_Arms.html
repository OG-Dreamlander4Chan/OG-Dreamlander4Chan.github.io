Empire of Roam: Firearms



Roam was already home to artisan machinery craftsponies before the war. Turning their inventiveness towards firearms, the Empire of Roam managed to produce some of the finest, highest quality arms int he NEIGHTO alliance. Willing to spend that extra bunch of bits to get exactly what they desire, Roaman weapons were quickly regarded for their robustness and overall feeling of quality - objects d'art in the Art of War.


"Nasser"
WEAPON TYPE: Assault Rifle
CALIBER: 9x35mm Orghuzan
CAPACITY: 30 Round Detachable Box Magazine
EFFECTIVE RANGE: 250 meters
BARREL LENGTH: 320mm/12.6 inches
OVERALL LENGTH: 800mm/31.5 inches
OVERALL HEIGHT: 220mm/8.66 inches
TOTAL MASS: 8.97lbs/4.06kg
Yet another iteration of the Patton design, the "Nasser" is chambered in the original 9x35mm, but designed for a MUCH higher pressure cartridge, much closer to the Earth caliber ".357 Maximum." This gives it impressive velocity - about 620 meters-per-second with a 10-gram bullet. The Spitzer design is much more pronounced on certain loadings, while others simply use a lengthened standard 9x35mm projectile. While they lack the range of the Bradley, they have better terminal performance against meaty targets.
"Abdul"
WEAPON TYPE: Sub-Machine Gun
CALIBER: 7.92x24mm Herdiet
CAPACITY: 32 Round Detachable Box Magazine
EFFECTIVE RANGE: 150 meters
EFFECTIVE RANGE: 100 meters
BARREL LENGTH: 260mm/10.25 inches
OVERALL LENGTH: 720mm/28.35 inches
OVERALL HEIGHT: 206mm/8.1 inches
TOTAL MASS: 7.1lbs/3.2kg
The Abdul is a direct Zhukov copy, chambered in 7.92mm Herdiet. The 7.92mm round has better range, better barrier penetration, and generally higher performance overall, here merged with the superior handling of the Zhukov.
