Kingdom of Navarre: Warships (Armada Real)



Navarre didn't have much of a Navy before the Civil War. They had royal sailing ships that kept the peace, but the fleet was very informal. This is in sharp contrast to Spain, the Earth Nation that Navarre would be, that once had a very powerful Armada. The Navarese Navy ("Armada Real") took parts from other nations, and combined them into different ships, often obtaining data packages for different weapon systems and things, so they could produce their own fleet.
Navarre uses a hull classification system similar to Prance and Ambleion, where hull numbers are assigned based on the ship's general classification (Capital Ship vs. Patrol Vessel, etc.). The ship prefix is "RNB" for "Real Naval Buque" - or, Royal Naval Vessel.

The two Tiede class vessels are copies of the Prench Jeanne d'Arc class, with Equestrian 55mm guns installed instead of Prench versions, and in Navarese hull colors. In every respect, they're just as capable. Some slight internal modifications were made for Navarese use, including reorganizing crew general areas, storage, and arrangement of crew quarters.

The gracefully long King Alfonso VIII class vessels are actually constructed on very long Cruiser hulls, not Battleship hulls. This does give them an armor disadvantage, as they simply do not have the same thickness of armor as true Battleship hulls would. However, this decreases total tonnage as well, and made the ships more easy to build in Navarese dockyards. Typefying the "fast and light" design principle that Navarre largely holds to, the ships are capable of an impressive 34.5 knots. The main guns are identical to those on the Roaman Impero Class battleships, and were purchased from Roam directly. The 55mm guns are in Equestrian mounts, but locally produced. The 25mm guns were purchased from Equestria, and the 5-inch guns are Prench.

The Galicia class heavy cruisers are pretty standard for their type, with 10 8-inch guns, and a decent AAA compliment. What sets them apart from most other heavy cruisers, is the addition of Torpedoes. This gives them the ability to take on Battleships. Once again, Navarre obtained their armament and designs from elsewhere. The main guns are Equestrian, as are the 55mm gun mounts. The hull is based on the Erie class. The superstructure is entirely their own design.

Navarese Light Cruisers have some unique features, including higher caliber main guns than most Light Cruisers, and a unique Anti-Submarine Weapon, the "Calamar" Mortar. The Mortar fires three large projectiles with magnetic sensor warheads, at a fairly rapid rate, in a fan-shaped pattern. If one of these mortal shells gets close enough to an enemy submarine, it explodes, and will likely set off the other nearby charges in the pattern, making a very large blast underwater.
