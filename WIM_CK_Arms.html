My Little Pony: Warfare is Magic

Crystal Kingdom: Firearms



The Crystal Kingdom simply adopted all existing Equestrian designs, but produced them locally. The Crystal Kingdom also adopted Equestria's Militia System, but had their own police force - the Royal Bureau of Investigation (R.B.I.) as a central criminal investigation force. This force (once rebuilt) was armed along Gendarmerie lines, but retained its name. Unique to the Crystal Kingdom are Aero Marines, which fuse the concept of Paratroopers and Marines together. In modern Earth terms, they have the "First Strike" responsibility of Marines, the airlift training of Air Cavalry, and the combat parachute training of Airborne forces.
