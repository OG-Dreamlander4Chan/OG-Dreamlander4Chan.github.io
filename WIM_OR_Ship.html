Sultanate of Orghuz: Warships (Royal Navy of the Sultan of Orghuz)



Orghuz has a very narrow selection of ships, and the ships themselves are less than impressive, as of late 1004 C.S. Fleet coordination is done through radio sets stolen and copied from Equestria, however, co-ordination seems to be rudimentary at best.
Orghuz uses the prefix "SDK" which seems to stand for, roughly, "Royal Navy Ship."


Royal Sultan-Class
CLASS NAME: Royal Sultan SHIPS IN CLASS: (Observed Only) Royal Sultan, Ataman I, Kulanmeni I
SHIP TYPE: Pre-Dreadnaught Battleship ENGINES: 20 Otosan Triple Drum Boilers & 4 Direct Gear Turbines SCREWS: 4 SPEED: (Observed) 24kts
PRIMARY ARMAMENT: 3 x 2 280mm/40cal SECONDARY ARMAMENT: None TERTIARY ARMAMENT: 8 x 1 100mm/45cal casemates MACHINE GUNS: None
OVERALL LENGTH: 120 meters/394 feet NUMBER OF DECKS: 5 Belowdeck, 1 Casemate Deck, 1 Superstructure, 1 Flying Bridge BEAM: 33 meters/108 feet DRAFT: 6.2 meters/20 feet
TRANSIT RANGE: ~1800 nmi TOTAL CREW: Estimated at 750 MARINE DETACHMENT: Not Known NUMBER OF BOATS: None Observed
Even fatter tubs than Germane vessels, the Royal Sultan class can shrug off even more punishment and remain in combat condition. During the Naval Battle of the Eyesore Islands, the Royal Sultan herself absorbed 12 armor-piercing 200lbs/90kg bombs from CFA-1 Fighter-Bombers and sustained no appreciable damage.
Sufan-Class
CLASS NAME: Sufan SHIPS IN CLASS: (Observed) Sufan, Agri, Dagi, Kucuk, Kisara, Emler
SHIP TYPE: Unprotected Cruiser ENGINES: 12 Otosan Triple Drum Boilers & 4 Direct Gear Turbines SCREWS: 4 SPEED: 28kts
PRIMARY ARMAMENT: 3 x 1 100mm/45cal SECONDARY ARMAMENT: 4 x 3 480mm Oxygen Torpedo TERTIARY ARMAMENT: None MACHINE GUNS: None
OVERALL LENGTH: 80 meters/262 feet NUMBER OF DECKS: 4 Belowdeck, 1 Superstructure, 1 Bridge BEAM: 20 meters/65 feet DRAFT: 6.2 meters/20 feet
TRANSIT RANGE: ~1400 nmi TOTAL CREW: Estimated at 400 MARINE DETACHMENT: Not Known NUMBER OF BOATS: 5 meter launches observed
Slim and short, these can barely be called cruisers. More like, Torpedo Ships. They have much thinner armor than the Battleships, and are clearly designed for hit-and-run strikes - a role severely limited by their slow speed. Canidan Frigates were able to gun these down with little trouble.
Yarmouk-Class
CLASS NAME: Yarmouk SHIPS IN CLASS: (Observed Only) Yarmouk, Ziqlab, Chorath, Kafranja, Zarqua, Sha'eb
SHIP TYPE: Torpedo Corvette ENGINES: 8 Otosan Triple Drum Boilers, 2 Direct Gear Turbines SCREWS: 2 SPEED: 28kts
PRIMARY ARMAMENT: 2 x 1 100mm/45cal SECONDARY ARMAMENT: 4 x 3 480mm Oxygen Torpedo TERTIARY ARMAMENT: None MACHINE GUNS: None
OVERALL LENGTH: 60 meters/197 feet NUMBER OF DECKS: 4 Belowdeck, 1 Conning Tower BEAM: 12 meters/40 feet DRAFT: 6.2 meters/20 feet
TRANSIT RANGE: ~900 nmi TOTAL CREW: Approximately 80 MARINE DETACHMENT: Up to 24 observed NUMBER OF BOATS: None observed
Thin and relatively quick, the Torpedo Corvettes of the Orghuz Navy suffer the same speed disadvantages as the rest of the fleet, but the fast and powerful oxygen torpedoes they use are nothing to sneeze at. They are, however, almost totally unarmored all over the place, and the superstructure can be penetrated with simple rifle fire from 7mm NEIGHTO ball rounds.
