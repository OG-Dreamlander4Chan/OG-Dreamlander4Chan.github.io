Empire of Roam: Firearms



Roam was already home to artisan machinery craftsponies before the war. Turning their inventiveness towards firearms, the Empire of Roam managed to produce some of the finest, highest quality arms int he NEIGHTO alliance. Willing to spend that extra bunch of bits to get exactly what they desire, Roaman weapons were quickly regarded for their robustness and overall feeling of quality - objects d'art in the Art of War.


Una Ungulam Telum, Modella 03 (UUT M-03)
WEAPON TYPE: Double Action Semi-Automatic Pistol
MANUFACTURERS: Pietro Boaretta, S.p.A.
CALIBER: 9x23mm NEIGHTO
CAPACITY: 12 + 1 Round Magazine
EFFECTIVE RANGE: 50 meters
BARREL LENGTH: 140mm/5.5 inches
OVERALL LENGTH: 224mm/8.81 inches
OVERALL HEIGHT: 182mm/7.16 inches
TOTAL MASS: 2.5lbs/1.13kg
Time was taken to create Roam's official sidearm, and the resulting Modella 03 is a fantastically well made weapon, though on the larger side, even for a Pony hoof. However, the firepower of 12 rounds is very handy for close quarters defense. "Una Ungulam Telum" means "One Hoof Weapon" in Old Roaman (Latin).
Impetum Ungulam Telum, Modella 03 (IUT M-03)
WEAPON TYPE: Sub-Machine Gun
MANUFACTURERS: Pietro Boaretta, S.p.A.
CALIBER: 9x23mm NEIGHTO
CAPACITY: 30 Round Detachable Box Magazine
EFFECTIVE RANGE: 150 meters
BARREL LENGTH: 210mm/8.26 inches
OVERALL LENGTH: 776mm/30.5 inches
OVERALL HEIGHT: 186mm/7.32 inches
TOTAL MASS: 9.2lbs/4.1kg
Based on the Ridgway SMG, the Impetum Ungulam Telum (Assault Hoof Weapon) Modella 03 is more refined, softer shooting, handles more like a rifle, and while heavy, is very well balanced with less recoil. The Roaman Navy issues this as their primary long arm, with only Marksponies having actual rifle caliber arms available. Given the close ranges on ships, a 150-meter range is generally more than enough to hit a target reliably at the bow of even the largest vessels, from the stern.
