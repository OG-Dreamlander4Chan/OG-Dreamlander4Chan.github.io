Kingdom of New Equestria: Warships



The New Equestrian Royal Navy suffers from the same rot, lack of ponypower, and funding issues as the rest of the New Equestrian Armed Forces. However, they have insanely powerful units and global reach, with the Global Prompt Strike missile system.
New Equestria still uses the same system they did during the Great Tirek War - all ships receive a prefix code and are titled "HMS" for "Her Majesty's Ship."

The most advanced ship ever made, the Wishing Star represents the pinnacle of naval strike technology, with a defensive and self-protection suite capable of tracking every incoming object as small as a bird, out to twice the effective range of the defensive system's response time, giving it it more than enough time to react to any incoming threat. However, the only unit was mothballed in 2040 C.S. and its sister ship sits at 45% complete and laid up, unfinished.
