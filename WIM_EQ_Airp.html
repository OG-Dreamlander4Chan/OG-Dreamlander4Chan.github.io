Kingdom of Equestria: Aircraft (Royal Equestrian Air Force)



Most nations had a "flying service" before the re-introduction of powered aircraft in 1003 C.S., but they exclusively consisted of Pegasi with specific training. The most famous of which, are Equestria's aerial acrobat elite, the "Wonderbolts." With the start of the war, talented Pegasi from all over trained to be Fighters, Bombers, and Transporters, thinking that only Pegasi would ever be able to take flight. With the restoration of the Crystal Kingdom, and the redevelopment of lost technology by Bright Eyes, Equestria produced the first powered combat aircraft, the F/A-1 Falcon, by 28 Lunarius, 1003 C.S.


F/A-1 Falcon
DESIGNATION: F/A-1 "Falcon" ROLE: Multirole Fighter/Strike Aircraft AIRCRAFT TYPE: Semi-Neutral Bouyancy, Lifting Body
GUN ARMAMENT: 3 x 1 11mm EM-4 Browning ORDNANCE ARMAMENT: Up to 500lbs of explosives HARD POINTS: 5 internal
OVERALL LENGTH: 6.5 meters/21.3 feet OVERALL HEIGHT: 2.36 meters/7.75 feet ENGINES: 1 Diamon-Merietta FR-1 Fractal Petroleum
CRUISE SPEED: 60kts/11kph/69mph MAXIMUM SPEED: 77kts/142kph/88mph FERRY RANGE: 1000 nmi
The very first "Fighter" aircraft, the F/A-1 Falcon made its debut in the failed Cowrean Intervention. Light, weakly armed, but none the less a boon to the battlefield, only about 120 were made before moving on to the F/A-2 Eagle. Armaments included 100lbs (45kg) freefall bombs, 250lbs (113kg) Armor-Piercing anti-ship bombs, and 70mm "Hydra" folding fin aerial rockets.
