My Little Pony: Marking Warfare's Tale



Chapter 6: Uniting the Crystal, Pt. 1

Part 1: Bridge Out

TIME: 09:12 NERST
DATE: Redemption Day 13/19 Septimus, 2054 C.S.
LOCATION: Hinterlands of Erie, New Equestria


We stayed in Cupcake Falls for the afternoon, helping clean things up, and helping sort through the corpses. It was a fascinating search. I was learning, and I would be adapting. Things were different, here. I had the north-eastern one fifth or so of Equestria to work with. That was it.

So, I would work with what I had.

We drove from Cupcake Falls towards the ruins of Princesston, staying south of the Ley Line breach. Really, of all things, I would have preferred some culturally enforced barrier of wilderness and wasteland, instead of actual barriers. That made everything so much harder.

Princesston had been a major city. The fact that it fell into ruin over the last 1,000 years was troubling, and I knew I'd never get a truly satisfactory answer.

Hitch, however, had to stop the JLTV.

Hitch: "Ah, K? Bridge is out." He pointed to the bridge Jasper had taken us across.

K: "Mmm.. any other bridges across the Hudson?" I nodded.

Hitch: "Mmm.. not for a way's south.. we'll have to go to Plowkeepsie." He shrugged.

K: "Alright." I nodded. "We have extra fuel, we should be okay." I looked back at everyone. Izzy was kinda vibrating in her seat. Pipp was doing something with her phone. Sunny was in the turret. Zipp was looking out the window, leaned out a bit, looking at the bridge. So, I looked back to Hitch. "No way to Ford the river, here?"

Hitch: "Nah, it's too deep, too wide, even with the snorkel kit." He shrugged. "There might be a shallower section up stream, but-" He looked back to Izzy, as if to ask.

Izzy: "Sorry, Hitch - I never really left Bridlewood. Well, except this one time? But I only went, like, a mile outta town, you know, into the woods - I saw something out there and I followed it. I almost didn't find my way back." She lit that off rapid-fire, with a beaming grin.

Misty was looking out the window, but smiling.

K: "Alright. Plowkeepsie it is." I nodded. "What's the place like?"

Hitch: "Very small, now. Lots of ruins around it - it's a Unicorn settlement, mainly. There's a Gendarmes team from Waterberry that comes over there every now and them." He shrugged.

K: "It used to be a big town." I sighed. "I remember, during the war, we trained up their Militia after the 2nd Battle of Ponyville. Golden Harvest did it. Harv.." I snorted, derisively, to keep the tears back.

Izzy: "..you still hear their spark.." She said softly, putting a hoof on my shoulder. "..it's okay, K."

K: *AHEM* "They're gone now, and I have to.. get my head right with that." I shook my head.

Misty: "You will." She smiled.

Hitch: "Let's go." He nodded, and off we drove.


TIME: 09:37 NERST
LOCATION: Plowkeepsie, Erie, New Equestria


We drove up to the hill that ringed the edge of town, and I could just about see down into it. I saw 2LT Iron Bar's LTV disabled near the center of town, with smoke coming out of it, and a firefight. The civilians were being systematically evacuated towards the bridge.

Misty: "OH NO!" She jammed her head out the window. "We have to help them!"

K: "No kidding." C> "Alpha Actual to any units that can hear my signal - Plowkeepsie is under attack by a large enemy force. Any and all units of any branch in range of this broadcast, respond with call sign." I brought my Manticore between my legs and got ready to use it. "And I thought Cupcake Falls was going to be the first dance - guess that was just the first song."

Zipp: "Hitch, get us there!" She pulled Sunny from the Turret and got up there herself. "Sunny, get ready to pick them off as we move - I'll handle volume."

Sunny: "Right, Zipp." She nodded, and braced her scoped Manticore at the corner of the window.

2LT Iron Bar: C> "Alpha, this is Golf - we have 7 ponies down, we're retreating, and we're evacuating civilians as we can."

K: C> "Roger, Golf." I held on as Hitch wordlessly gunned the engine and launched us off the hill.

Capt. Silver Shock: C> "This is Phoenix Two-Two, Alpha. We hear your broadcast."

That was an Air Force callsign. I was about to respond, but Zipp ducked down and grabbed the mic from me.

Zipp: C> "This is Alpha Three - Silver Shock, we need close air support. Will mark friendlies with smoke. Infantry forces in the open and building cover. Requesting Gun Run." She looked at me and shrugged.

K: "Yeah, you got this." I nodded as we started to get to the town itself.

Capt. Silver Shock: C> "Phoenix Two-Two, with Phoenix Flight, Gun Run roger. Friendlies ID'd with smoke."

Zipp: C> "Phoenix, roger." She keyed up Iron Bar. C> "Golf, this is Alpha Three, mark friendlies with smoke - Phoenix CAS ready with gun run."

2LT Iron Bar: C> "Roger, Alpha Three."

Capt. Lon Scones: C> "Alpha, Alpha, this is 18th Air Cav SAR Team - callsign Angel One. How copy, over?"

I took the mic back, as Zipp was soon occupied shooting. I saw them at the windows, and I couldn't - didn't - want to address what they were.

K: C> "One-Eight, Angel One, this is Actual. What have you got for me?"

Capt. Lon Scones: C> "Eight Scouts, three Heavies, we are armed. ETA 8 minutes."

K: C> "That'll do. Friendlies marked with Smoke." I called back.

Capt. Lon Scones: C> "Roger."

Hitch: "What IS it with these nasties?!" He swerved, to avoid one, but it jumped at, and grabbed the door... and put a hand over Hitch's face.

K: "HEAD. BACK." I yelled in his ear, as I cleared leather with my Roc.

He slammed his head back into the seat, and I stuck my arm all the way out, putting it to the monster's head.

Raptorian: *SQRAWWK!* "THE HUMAN LIVES! IMPO-"

*PACHAMM!*

He fell away as a 150gr flatnose tore through its skull, obliterating its bird brain.

Hitch: "Thanks!" He nodded, spitting out a mouthful of feathers.

K: "HEAD FOR THE RIVER! We'll cover the civilians!" I grabbed the mic. C> "Iron Bar, fall back to the river as you can! We're covering your back door!"

2LT Iron Bar: C> "Roger!"

Misty: "These.. these a-are Raptorians!" She spoke up, finally.

K: "Yeah, they sure are!" I yelled back. "HITCH! Hold it up!" I said, kicking the door open as he hit the brakes. "Sunny! Cover the left! Zipp, cover from up top!" I said, jumping out.

My knees bitched. I wasn't in shape for this. But, General K was screaming inside of me - I had to help. I would not leave anypony behind. I ran for the structure we had stopped by - I heard a foal screaming inside, and the place was on fire. I chugged to the door, and heaved my body through it. Adrenaline rushed through my veins, burning its path through my body.

I was missing this.

I stomped up the stairs, body still not used to this exertion, and trying to keep my weapon ahead of me. I heard hoof-falls behind me, and I turned as I got to the top of the stairs.

Misty.

K: "What are you doing!?" I demanded.

Misty: "H-Helping!" She stammered, clearly terrified, but refusing to let it stop her as she galloped up the stairs.

K: "Right, stay behind me." I couldn't order her to leave. "ROYAL ARMY, CALL OUT IF YOU CAN HEAR US!" I paused. "ROYAL ARMY, CALL OUT!"

Foal: *AIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIEEEEEEEEEEEEEAAAAAAAAAAAAH!!*

K: "Far back room." I forced my legs to piston across the hallway, and once again, lacking grace, I just jumped at the last minute and let my ponderous bulk of 280lbs break down the door. I rolled with it, and saw two Raptorians trying to stuff a peach-colored foal into a sack.

Raptorian: "HUMAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAN!" It screeched.

Peach Fizz: *Sobbing* "AW-HAW-HAW! HELP MEEEEEE!"

K: "PUT THE KID DOWN, YOU MOTHER FUCKING PIECE OF FEATHERED SHIT, OR I'M GOING TO GO HUMAN ON YOUR SWEET-AND-SOUR, GO-WELL-WITH-KETCHUP, CHICKEN NUGGET, MOTHER FUCKING, NEST SNIFFING, FEATHER... DUSTER.. ASSSSSS!" I screamed a tirade..

..because I know. Knew. Hurty words break brains in this world. Another "magic" that some didn't know - psychology. My body wasn't made for this any more, my precision wouldn't be good enough to hit the bird without hitting the foal.. so, I had to reach into my bag of tricks and pull something out.

The Raptorians indeed froze. And covered their ears to not hear my curse words. Comically, one of them even plugged the Foal's ears with his fingers. Even Misty covered her ears.

Raptorian: "You kiss mother with that mouth, human!?" He was shocked, and released the foal without realizing it.

K: "When you get to hell, ask 'er." Perfect shot, lined up, fired, hyperburst, Raptorian head exploded.

Misty had a sigil drawn on the ground already, and two tendrils of gold fabric came out, and snatched the foal before the second Raptorian could grab her.

I swiveled around to the side, and put two hyper bursts into the second Raptorian's chest. He exploded like a pillow under a firecracker.

K: "OUT! GO!" I forced myself up, and pushed them both ahead of me.

This Sendak wanted a fight? Fine, I'll give it to him - me and my military might be out of shape, but we can still punch so far above our weight, we could - and would - give him a black eye for trying to snuff me out before I can get us back together.

No matter what.

[End of Part 1]

Part 2: Battle of Plowkeepsie, Pt. 1 - Rescue Ops

TIME: 10:17 NERST
DATE: Redemption Day 13/19 Septimus, 2054 C.S.
LOCATION: Hinterlands of Erie, New Equestria

As we got outside, I got a look at what our air cover was: Dusters. That is, propeller-driven, usually single engine, small air planes with machine guns and light bombs. Perfect for counter-insurgency fighting.

One flew overhead, did a shallow dive, then executed a gun run. One Raptorian took to the skies, but the Duster lined them up and shot them down in short order.

Misty dove into the vehicle with the little foal in her forelegs, and I re-mounted the passenger side door, forcing myself into it half-way, hanging on to the grab bar inside it, and watching for more targets.

Hitch: "What's the plan, K?" He leaned over and asked, as he hit the gas.

K: "Iron Bar has the right idea - get everyone evacuated to the far side of the river. Hold the bridge. Maybe we can take 'em." I looked back a bit to see Pipp cuddling the foal. "How's the little one?"

Pipp: "She's okay!" She smiled. "You really do care, huh?"

K: "I had foals myself, once." I said, letting the gravity of the next turn slide me into my seat, and I pulled the door shut.

Sunny blushed a bit, smiling.

Hitch: "And what are WE doing?" He asked, looking over to me.

K: "Seeing to the safety of the civilians!" I nodded.

Zipp: "That's fine - next time don't go alone!" She scolded me. "We can't afford to lose you - we just got you back!"

K: "I think that's part of the point, Zipp! We're being HERDED into danger. Whoever this is, wants me dead, and doesn't want us to bring the magic back!" I stated, as I took a few suppressive fire shots at some Raptorians I saw passing by.

Peach Fizz: "Bring MAGIC back?!" She was stunned.

Pipp: "That's right, Pippsqueak." She immediately launched into her Online Persona. "We. Are going to bring ALL.. the magic back. And it's going to be faaabuloooouuus!" She sang that last word, and while I didn't see it, she gave her new fan a small, marketable plushie of herself.

Peach Fizz: "My mom told me that's impossible!" She sounded almost breathless.

Pipp: "T'ch'ya, it WAS.. but we have K the Human back." She waved a hoof at me. "He can do it. He can do anything."

Peach Fizz: "Oooooooooooohhhhh..! THAT'S who you are!" She giggled.

K: "Yep, little one, I'm the Human of Legend. Undefeated. Killer of a thousand monsters. Slayer of Tirek the Tyrant." I nodded, speaking in platitudes and boasts to reassure her. "But it's you ponies that have the magic in you - I'm just here to help make it possible."

Peach Fizz: "Aw, alright!" She smiled. "You can do it!"

Pipp: "WE can do it." She pat her head. "Together."

Hitch: "That house is on fire." He said, casually, pulling to a stop.

K: "Yeah, gonna check that." I pushed my way out of the passenger side door. "Misty." I motioned for her to follow me. "Cover me."

Hitch: "Yeah, I'm going with you." He pushed the driver's side door open. "Pipp, take over."

Pipp: "Ahh... oooookkaayyy..!" She said, nervously. "I think I can remember how to do this!"

We ran into the burning building. I was being a fool, but I knew civilians needed me. I didn't care. Pain, loss, maybe death - if I couldn't do this, I didn't deserve these ponies.

Hitch: "GENDARMES, CALL OUT! CALL OUT IF YOU CAN HEAR US!" He was on his hind legs, Titan's Mitt gripping his Manticore.

Seashell: "WE'RE UP HERE!" The little filly's voice shouted from the second floor.

K: "Hitch." I patted his back and let him take point.

Misty: "I'm right behind you." She said, her voice a bit shaky, but determined.

We charged up the steps, with me chugging along, huffing and puffing in the heat. At the top of the stairs, a Raptorian broke through the window. Hitch shoulder-checked him, and he was tossed back out, as I pivoted and kicked in the door to the room where the foals were.

Misty: "Fire!" She said, breathlessly, before lowering herself to the ground, and grabbing a potion from her saddlebags. "Hold on." She hefted it into the room, and everything on fire, turned to icicles.

Hitch: "HEY! That's pretty good!" He said, moving in behind us.

Glory: "We're here!" She squirmed out from under the bed with the other foal, Seashell. "They're burning down our house!" She whined.

Hitch: "It's okay, little ones - Sheriff Hitch is here!" He grabbed Seashell up.

K: "Misty, get the other one." I pointed.

Misty: "Right!" She slid under Glory, and got her up on her back.

K: "Back to the Jeep!" I ordered.

As we exited the room, that SAME RAPTORIAN got back in through the window and grabbed ME around the shoulders as Hitch and Misty got down the stairs. I was about to scream, but I felt myself pulled backwards.

Instinctively, I released my Manticore, and grabbed my combat knife, stabbing the Raptorian in the arm, then over my shoulder, getting him in the neck. He kicked off of me, but I was in freefall..

..my landing was broken by an orange-coated stallion.

Orange Rind: "ACK! WHAT THE TARTARUS!?" *OOF!*

We were in a pile, on the ground, on the street behind the house. And there were Raptorians circling us. I managed to roll off of him, and get to my knees.

Orange Rind: "..of all the luck." He grimaced, getting to his hooves.

K: "I think we're in trouble." I simply stated.

Orange Rind: "Mmm.. they don't have guns." He flicked an eye to me.

K: "Point taken." I nodded, getting fully to my feet, as the Raptorians circled us - five of them. "You wouldn't happen to know Praying Mantis, would you?" I chuckled.

Orange Rind: "Don't need it." His eyes narrowed.

K: "Good enough." I got into a knife fighting stance. "COME ON, YOU FEATHER DUSTERS!"

So, I'm in an alleyway, surrounded by five Raptorians, with a gruff but handsome stallion of significant build.. I have a knife, they have claws, he has hooves. Before I could try to entice one of them into striking, he charged forwards, wheeled around at the last second, kicked one of them into the wall, then rapidly back-kicked into him so fast, I couldn't see the individual hoof strikes, landing one at a time, but I heard the machine-gun pace of hooves striking bone, breaking bone, and tenderizing the meat.

The Raptorian, when the assault was complete, was mush. And it just slid down the wall, leaving a huge streak of blood.

Orange Rind: *Snort* "Useless!" He looked at the remaining four.

K: "Who's next?" I puffed myself up.

Right about then, Pipp, driving the JLTV, came swerving around the corner..

Pipp: "GET IN!" She shrieked.

Orange Rind: "..eh?"

K: "Come ON!" I grabbed him, and threw him in the now-open back hatch, diving in myself. "HIT IT!"

With that, Pipp took off.. and with MUCH LESS control than Hitch had.

Orange Rind: "WATCH IT!" He yelled out. "CAN YOU EVEN DRIVE?!"

Pipp: "Not REALLLLYYYYYY!" She shrieked.

Hitch: "HANG ON!" He yelled out as we got on to the main road.

K: "Head for the bridge!" I yelled.

As we careened down the street with our new acquaintance and the three saved foals, I had no idea how this battle was going to play out. It did, however, just get VERY interesting.

[End of Part 2]


Part 3: Battle of Plowkeepsie, Pt. 2 - Battle at the Big Bridge

TIME: 10:42 NERST
DATE: Redemption Day 13/19 Septimus, 2054 C.S.
LOCATION: Plowkeepsie, Erie, New Equestria


Pipp.. is not a good driver. But, that's alright - she slammed the JLTV into five Raptorians trying to keep the vehicle on the road. We were a bit over encumbered, too, and I'd lost my rifle falling out of the back of the house like that. So, I grabbed the bungie straps at the back of the JLTV's cargo hatch, and attached them to the retaining clips on my gear.

They were supposed to be able to handle up to 350lbs. I was about 280 in street clothes.

Somewhat dangling out the back, I managed to get my Roc out, and take a few pot shots at some Raptorians as we passed them by. Picking one shot here and there. I wasn't expecting to be precise at these speeds, but I managed to score 4 of 15 hits.

K: "HOW MUCH FURTHER!?" I called back.

Pipp: "ALMOST THERE!" She called back.

We hit another Raptorian, and it flew over the top of the JLTV. At the last second, it grabbed the edge of the rear cargo tailgate, and started trying to crawl up towards me. I was in the middle of a magazine change. So, I extended my leg slowly - so I could get to the magazines on my thigh - pressed my boot against its face, and grabbed a fresh magazine.

K: "Wait your turn." I stated, calmly.

Raptorian: "DESTROY HUMAN! FOR GREAT SENDAK!" It screeched.

I got the magazine in, and moved my boot away from its face. It grasped for my leg, I leaned a bit forwards, and pressed the barrel to its head, between its eyes, and pulled the trigger. Its bird brains blew out the back of its skull.

Glory: "HERE, MISTER!" She slid Hitch's Manticore to me.

K: "Thanks, lil' one!" I grabbed it, and got it to my shoulder, bending one knee, and aiming roughly as a flock of Raptorians landed in the street behind me.

Seashell: "NASTY MONSTERS! GO AWAYYYY!" She screeched.

K: "Yeah, they're going away alright." I knew Hitch had his full auto set slower than mine, but I switched it over to that, and pulled the trigger, raking the lines behind me as best I could.

Orange Rind: "Of all the luck." I heard him curse, almost next to me, given the cramped conditions.

K: "Bad day, bad day, bad day, bad day.." I chanted, reloading the Manticore.

Hitch: "K!" He leaned back. "Iron Bar says he can't hold the bridge! They're got too many of them! And the Dusters are bingo fuel!"

K: "What does he want?!" I called back.

Hitch: "He wants permission to blow the bridge!" Hitch nodded back at me.

I calculated everything in my head. Fast as ever. I could see the entire battle space, mentally - I knew the civilians were more important than anything. Given the large scale of the attack, we'd need a larger scale response to throw them out of here.

K: "DO IT IF HE CAN! PIPP, TURN SOUTH! GET US OUT OF TOWN!" I called to her.

Pipp: "A-ALRIGHT! HANG ON!" She turned the wheel.

We all kinda slid to the side. My finger came off the Manticore's trigger, and I ended up with Peach Fizz tumbling into my lap. I grabbed her with my free arm.

Peach Fizz: "Don't let go!" She squeaked.

K: "Never!" I tucked her almost into the small of my back, leaned forwards a bit, and braced my right foot on the lip of the tailgate.

As Pipp turned, we came to the street that ran parallel to the bridge. And I got to see just how bad it was. Raptorians everywhere. Sendak seemed to control all the races that Tirek once had. What the TARTARUS did my friends back then, even die for!?

Pipp hit the brakes suddenly, though. I felt myself pulled back, and quickly slid the toe of my boot under the cargo loop to arrest my motion, so I wouldn't squish the foal.

Zipp: "K?! WHAT THE TARTARUS IS THAT THING!?" She screeched from the turret.

I stood up, detaching one of the bungee cords, and looked up over the back edge of the JLTV.

What I saw was something new. Very new. It was at least ten feet tall, broad, with four arms and two legs. Its body form reminded me of a salamander that took steroids, hit the gym, then started beating up nerds for lunch money. It wore metal plated armor, and it carried two Abrams machine guns.

K: "I.. have no idea." I blinked.

X-Acto: "AHAHAHAHAHH!" It bellowed, loosing a few shots into the air. "I'm X-Acto! Field Commander for Lord Sendak! AND YOU ARE MY PREY!"

K: "Pipp." I leaned forwards and into the edge of the turret ring.

Pipp: "Y-Y-Yeah, K?!" She stammered, very afraid.

K: "Gun the engine, aim for a knee, knock this guy over." I said, calmly, grabbing the bungee strap I'd detached, and reattaching it.

I then grabbed the last strap, wrapped it around my waist quickly, and cinched it to one of the cargo loops in the roof of the cargo deck. I reached forwards and unlocked Hitch's launcher, to see a Napalm grenade loaded. Perfect. I pulled the tube back into position, and looked up at the big lizard.

K: "I AM GENERAL K! SLAYER OF A THOUSAND MONSTERS, DESTROYER OF TIREK THE TYRANT! AND YOU ARE MY BITCH!" I aimed up, and fired the Napalm Grenade at his face, causing flames to erupt all over him.

Orange Rind: "ARE YOU TRYING TO PISS HIM OFF!?" He shouted up at me.

K: "Yeup." I smirked.

Pipp gunned the engine at that point, and accelerated, slamming into X-Acto's left knee, with the right front bumper, and taking his leg out. He fell on his face, but not before dropping one of his machine guns, which just about hit me in the face, as it landed on the roof.

I slid the Manticore into the JLTV, and grabbed the Abrams.

Hitch: "Don't stop, Pipp! KEEP GOING!" He urged her.

Izzy: "THAT guy had NO sparkle AT ALL!" She commented offhand.

Pipp: "I'm going, I'm going!" She put her hoof to the floor, and we were careening down the streets.

Soon enough, Big Boy was right behind us.

K: C> "Any air assets at ALL, this is Alpha Actual - we got a big bugger bearing down on us, we could use some support!" I called into the mic on my vest, as I cradled the Abrams.

Angel One-Five: C> "This is Angel One-Five. Coming by for a gun run!"

One of the scout helicopters - I'd need to see a list of these things - armed with a machine gun on one side, and a 130mm rocket pod on the other side, swung down over us, flanked us, and started firing on the monster with its 11mm machine gun. The monster wasn't having a good time with that, and that caused it to stop, and start trying to shoot the helicopter with its remaining Abrams.

I twisted around, still standing, still strapped into the JLTV, and fired a few short bursts into X-Acto, like.. remember me, motherfucker?

As we grew more distant, the helicopter bobbed, weaved, got some altitude, and sent its remaining 130mm rocket into the ground right at X-Acto's feet. He vanished in a cloud of smoke.

K: "THAT'S HOW WE DO IT!" I cheered. "Alright, Pipp.. head south, get us out of town. Hitch, signal Iron Bar - tell him to get transport for the Civilians, to Bridlewood and anyplace else that can take them in. We'll have to come back here and take the city back."

Hitch: "Got it. Just so you know, there's only one river crossing left.." He said, with a bit of nervousness. "It's down.. by The Remnant."

Everyone in the JLTV grew still, even our new guest.

K: "Is there a reas-"

Orange Rind: "You don't go to The Remnant unless you're looking for trouble." He butt in.

K: "You got some real bitterness to ya, stranger. What'cher name?" I nodded to him.

Orange Rind: "Orange Rind." He grunted.

K: "Fits." I looked at the foals. "And who are YOU three sweethearts?"

Peach Fizz: "I'm Peach Fizz!" She giggled. "I'm 10!"

Glory: "I'm Glory!" She popped up from between Izzy and Zipp. "I'm 10, too!"

Seashell: "I'm Seashell." She was on the other side of Izzy. "I'm ALSO 10!" She giggled, her head now sporting one of our spare helmets.

K: "We'll get you reunited with your folks real soon, kids." I nodded. "Hitch, give Iron Bar a Supplemental and let him know these three are safe."

Hitch: "Got it." He nodded, and got on the radio.

With that, we were heading out of town. Our ride was cramped, but we had new friends, including three ADORABLE fillies.. and a bitter stallion named Orange Rind.. who, I didn't know at the time.. was about to be a VERY important figure in the war.

[End of Part 3]


Part 4: Unprocessed

TIME: 11:11 NERST
DATE: Redemption Day 13/19 Septimus, 2054 C.S.
LOCATION: Southern Erie, New Equestria

{Field Report #1}

Pipp appears on screen, uniform a bit ripped up, very dirty, and quite wet. Her hair is matted down with grimy water. She looks a bit embarrassed and nervous.

Orange Rind: "SHE WRECKED IT COMPLETLEY!" He huffed, and kicked the rear fender.

Pipp OUT!"

She shut off the camera, sighed, and sank down on to her ass.

K: "That wasn't easy." I shrugged, sitting next to her.

Hitch: "K!" He trotted over. "Engine's had it. It doesn't help, we're almost out of fuel, too." He shook his head. "Looks like we sprang a leak sometime during the fight."

K: "How far is it to The Remnant?" I nodded to him, calmly.

Hitch: "Mmm.. if we push it, we can be there in.. two days?" He shrugged. "You're averaging about 2.4km per hour for about nine hours a day. I don't think the foals can go much faster than that." He gestured.

K: "Alright. Secure food and water." I nodded, and passed him my Manticore magazines. "Here. I'll carry the Abrams."

Hitch: "Alright." He filled his magazine pouches, and went to give his spare to Zipp.

Pipp: "We're gonna be okay, right?" She blinked at me.

K: "We'll be fine." I nodded. "We just have a long walk ahead of us, that's all."

Zipp: "Yeah." She paced up to me, and put some of the salvaged gear down, which we both started sorting through. "That's a full machine gun, then." She nodded to the Abrams
