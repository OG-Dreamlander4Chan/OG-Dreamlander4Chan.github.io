My Little Pony: Warfare is Magic

Kingdom of Equestria: Ground Vehicles



Motorized carriages were, at best, a novelty when the war began. Engine technology was usually some form of shrunk-down steam engine. After the rescue of the Crystal Kingdom, and Bright Eyes, miniaturization of Fractal Petroleum (Diesel) engines became possible, leading to the development of tanks. Tirek had already done so, somehow, though his armored vehicles were powered by Petroleum (Petrol/Gas). The fielding of "Tirek Heavy Tanks" at the same time as the first engagement of the M-1 led, eventually, to the discontinuation of "Tank Classes" and the development of the Main Battle Tank.


M-1 Tassigny
DESIGNATION: Motorcarriage 1, Tassigny ROLE: Medium Tank ARMOR: 178mm (Glacis), 127mm (Front Face), 100mm (Sides), 50mm (Rear), 12.5mm (E. Deck)
MAIN GUN: 76x320mm Gun MACHINE GUN: 2 x 11mm EM-4 Browning (1 Pintile, 1 Coax) AMMUNITION: 70 76mm Shells, 1200 11mm Rounds
OVERALL LENGTH: 7.6 meters/25 feet OVERALL HEIGHT: 3.44 meters/11.3 feet ENGINES: 2 Continental AGE-1 V-12 Fractal Petroleum
POWER TO WEIGHT RATIO: 11.5kw/tonne | 13.7hp/t MAXIMUM SPEED: 57kph/35mph (On Road) TRAVEL RANGE: 160 km
Equestria's first tank, the Tassigny was an experiment in how to create an armored vehicle fit for ponies. It worked in its job well enough, but the 76mm gun was found to be underpowered against Tirek Heavy Tanks, and the armor somewhat thin compared to their 150mm short-barrel howitzers. As such, Tassigny's were soon re-armed with the more powerful 77mm Tank Gun (76.2x560mm).
M-2 Powell
DESIGNATION: Motorcarriage 2, Powell ROLE: Heavy Tank ARMOR: 203mm (Glacis), 155mm (Front Face), 127mm (Sides), 90mm (Rear), 25mm (E. Deck)
MAIN GUN: 90x550mm Gun MACHINE GUN: 2 x 11mm EM-4 Browning (1 Pintile, 1 Coax) AMMUNITION: 70 76mm Shells, 1200 11mm Rounds
OVERALL LENGTH: 8.72 meters/28 feet OVERALL HEIGHT: 3.4 meters/11.15 feet ENGINES: 2 Continental AGE-1 V-12 Fractal Petroleum
POWER TO WEIGHT RATIO: 9.5kw/tonne | 12.74hp/t MAXIMUM SPEED: 42kph/26mph (On Road) TRAVEL RANGE: 160 km
Heavier than the Tassigny, but using the same power pack and drive train, the Powell is a beefier beast, with thicker armor and a much more powerful main gun - the same kind used on the HCMAS Superb, but with a shorter barrel. Issues with the power pack and tread pressure eventually contributed to the decision to move to Main Battle Tanks. On firm ground, however, the Powell is a serious foe to contend with. Tests on captured enemy Heavy Tanks showed the 90mm Gun could penetrate them at ranges up to 1km (angle depending), whereas the 76mm Gun can only penetrate at point-blank range.
M-3 Buford
DESIGNATION: Motorcarriage 3, Buford ROLE: Cavalry Fighting Vehicle ARMOR: 75mm (Front Face & Sides), 40mm (Rear), 20mm (Hatches)
MAIN GUN: 40x300mm Gun MACHINE GUN: 1 x 7mm EM-2 Abrams (Pintile, Commander), 1 x 11mm EM-4 Browning (Pintile, Troop Hatch) AMMUNITION: 60 40mm Shells, 600 11mm Rounds, 600 7mm Rounds
OVERALL LENGTH: 6.92 meters/22.7 feet OVERALL HEIGHT: 2.7 meters/7.21 feet ENGINES: 1 Continental AGE-1 V-12 Fractal Petroleum
POWER TO WEIGHT RATIO: 12.5kw/tonne | 16.8hp/t MAXIMUM SPEED: 75kph/46.6mph (On Road) TRAVEL RANGE: 180 km
This armored morsel is Equestria's bread and butter for transporting troops. Skipping over the "machine gun only APC" generation of armored warfare, this realtively heavily armed Cavalry Fighting Vehicle can carry up to 9 Troops with full equipment, but normally carries only 6. It requires a Driver, Gunner, and Commander. A section of 7 of these vehicles are assigned to each of three Cavalry Scouts Companies of a Hussar Regiment.
M-4 Meade
DESIGNATION: Motorcarriage 4, Meade ROLE: Light (Airborne) Tank ARMOR: 75mm (Front Face & Sides), 40mm (Rear), 20mm (Hatches)
MAIN GUN: 57x430mm Gun MACHINE GUN: 2 x 11mm EM-4 Browning (Pintile, Coax) AMMUNITION: 60 57mm Shells, 900 11mm Rounds
OVERALL LENGTH: 7.4 meters/24.27 feet OVERALL HEIGHT: 2.7 meters/7.21 feet ENGINES: 1 Continental AGE-1C V-12 Fractal Petroleum
POWER TO WEIGHT RATIO: 12.5kw/tonne | 16.8hp/t MAXIMUM SPEED: 75kph/46.6mph (On Road) TRAVEL RANGE: 200 km
The M-4 Meade was designed to be an Airborne Armored Vehicle almost from the start. Using an adjusted M-3 Buford chassis, the vehicle was given extra internal rubber bushings to fight the shock of a parachute drop. While this raised the weight, the improved performance of the Continental AGE-1C Engine evened it out. It has a long range for a vehicle of this type, mainly owing to the Troop Space being used for an enlarged fuel tank, and cargo. Its secondary mission is to carry extra ammunition, food, and equipment for the Airborne Infantry.
M-5 Wheeler
DESIGNATION: Motorcarriage 5, Wheeler ROLE: Reconnaisence Vehicle ARMOR: 75mm (Front Face & Sides), 40mm (Rear), 20mm (Hatches)
MAIN GUN: 20x125mm Gun MACHINE GUN: 1 x 11mm EM-4 Browning (Pintile) AMMUNITION: 85 20mm Shells, 600 11mm Rounds
OVERALL LENGTH: 6.7 meters/22 feet OVERALL HEIGHT: 2.7 meters/7.21 feet ENGINES: 1 Continental AGE-1C V-12 Fractal Petroleum
POWER TO WEIGHT RATIO: 12.5kw/tonne | 16.8hp/t MAXIMUM SPEED: 75kph/46.6mph (On Road) TRAVEL RANGE: 260 km
The M-5 Wheeler is assigned as the primary vehicle for every Reconnaisence Company. With some of the rubber bushing reinforcement of the M-4, an enlarged gas tank, and part of the troop section shortened to accomodate only 3-5 Troops (standard is 3), the Wheeler can go very long distances, especially if Jerry Cans of fuel are carried in the "wet" cargo compartment (the compartment is jacketed with a sealed water and ethylene glycol mixture).
M-17 Edson
DESIGNATION: Motorcarriage 17, Edson ROLE: Amphibious Assualt Vehicle ARMOR: 55mm (Front Face & Sides), 30mm (Rear), 25mm (Hatches)
MAIN GUN: 20x125mm Gun MACHINE GUN: 1 x 11mm EM-4 Browning (Pintile) AMMUNITION: 85 20mm Shells, 600 11mm Rounds
OVERALL LENGTH: 8 meters/26.5 feet OVERALL HEIGHT: 3.6 meters/11.8 feet ENGINES: 1 Continental AGE-1R V-12 Fractal Petroleum
POWER TO WEIGHT RATIO: 12kw/tonne | 16hp/t MAXIMUM SPEED: 60kph/37.3mph (On Road) TRAVEL RANGE: 240 km
The M-17 AAAV is designed to be a landing tank to support Marine infantry assualts. Equestria exported these to most other NEIGHTO nations. The Edson can carry 12 Troops, and has a 20mm cannon and an 11mm machine gun. It also mounts smoke grenade launchers. Its armor isn't very thick, but it can resist small arms fire, and light cannons along the front and sides.
