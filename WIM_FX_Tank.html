Princnipality of Vulpesylvania: Ground Vehicles



The Vulpes had very little industry at the start of the war, and a VERY mountainous terrain to defend. Ideal for infantry, but a nightmare for tanks, the Vulpes developed a single universal Armored Vehicle Chassis, and multiple different turrets for it, as its primary mechanized force, along with towed artillery that could be pulled by vehicle or by fox power alone. Light weight was the name of the game. As a result, most Vulpine units do not have much armor, though the Vulpes began development of a true Main Battle Tank/Heavy Tank for use once the invasion of Tirek's homelands began.


UCV-01 Athína
DESIGNATION: Universal Chassis Vehicle 01 "Athína" ROLE: Light/Medium Tank ARMOR: [Turret] 178mm (Glacis), 127mm (Front Face), 100mm (Sides/Rear)
[Hull] 44mm (Front Face), 36mm (Sides), 24mm (Rear), 18mm (E. Deck)
MAIN GUN: 76.2x560mm (77mm) HV Gun MACHINE GUN: 2 x 11mm EM-4 Browning (1 Pintile, 1 Coax) AMMUNITION: 62 77mm Rounds, 600 11mm Rounds
OVERALL LENGTH: 8.26 meters/27 feet OVERALL HEIGHT: 3.28 meters/10.7 feet ENGINES: 2 Jelcz FSB-1 w. 2 V-12 Fractal Petroleum
POWER TO WEIGHT RATIO: 13.4 kW/t | 18.3 hp/t MAXIMUM SPEED: 67.6kph/42mph (On Road) TRAVEL RANGE: 180 km
The first variant of the UCV was a medium tank, armed with a copied Equestrian Tassigny tank turret and the updated 77mm HV gun, making it very effective against most armored vehicles fielded by Tirek. The hull armor isn't great, but tank crews were taught to minimize exposure of the hull as best as possible, and use the terrain to their best advantage. As Vulpesylvania is very mountainous, and has few long open plains fit for tank combat, the 77mm HV gun is plenty of punch. These are assigned to Mechanized Infantry Brigades in a dedicated Armored Battalion in two Regiments.

POWER TO WEIGHT RATIO: 14 kW/t | 18.75 hp/t MAXIMUM SPEED: 73kph/45mph (On Road) TRAVEL RANGE: 245 km
The "Sparta" CFV has both a powerful autocannon with a 120-round-per-minute rate of fire, and a 3-trooper capacity. The extra space in the back is used for extra survival gear, and extra fuel, allowing the CFV's to be used in a scout capacity. One Regiment of Cav Scouts are attached to every Mechanized Infantry Brigade, and two are intended to be attached to every (heavy) Armored Brigade. The 40mm "Royal Engineering Research and Development Establishment, Messene" cannon is fed from a 4-shell clip, of which two can be mounted into the gun's cassette at a time.

[Hull] 44mm (Front Face), 36mm (Sides), 24mm (Rear), 18mm (E. Deck)

OVERALL LENGTH: 6.6 meters/21.65 feet OVERALL HEIGHT: 3.06 meters/10 feet ENGINES: 2 Jelcz FSB-1 w. 2 V-12 Fractal Petroleum
POWER TO WEIGHT RATIO: 14 kW/t | 18.75 hp/t MAXIMUM SPEED: 73kph/45mph (On Road) TRAVEL RANGE: 190 km
The APC variant of the UCV holds 6 troops with some spare room for survival gear, extra ammo, or even some extra fuel cans. It can be converted into an HQ Vehicle with minimal work, as there are attachment points for radio sets, and plugs, on the inside. In an emergency capacity, it could hold 8-9 soldiers, or up to 12 with no combat packs. The minimal armor is only proofed agaisnt 11mm AP rounds, and shrapnel splinters.
UCV-04 Thíva
DESIGNATION: Universal Chassis Vehicle 04 "Thíva" ROLE: Assault Gun/Tank Destroyer ARMOR: [Turret] 160mm (Front Face), 120mm (Sides)
[Hull] 44mm (Front Face), 36mm (Sides), 24mm (Rear), 18mm (E. Deck)
MAIN GUN: 1 x 105mm L7 Short MACHINE GUNS: 2 x 11mm Zeus Heavy Machine Gun (Pintile, Coax) AMMUNITION: 27 105mm Rounds, 600 11mm rounds
OVERALL LENGTH: 8.26 meters/27 feet OVERALL HEIGHT: 2.76 meters/9.05 feet ENGINES: 2 Jelcz FSB-1 w. 2 V-12 Fractal Petroleum
POWER TO WEIGHT RATIO: 10.4 kW/t | 14 hp/t MAXIMUM SPEED: 73kph/37mph (On Road) TRAVEL RANGE: 150 km
The Assault Gun/TD version of the UCV uses a short version of the L7 Tank Gun used on the Equestrian M16 Scott. This does reduce its effective range by 33%, but within its range envelope, it's quite deadly to enemy armor. Given how the nation of Vulpesylvania is laid out, using these as ambush vehicles is also viable, setting traps for incoming enemy armor and attacking them as they pass through narrow areas.
UCV-05 Siracusa
DESIGNATION: Universal Chassis Vehicle 04 "Thíva" ROLE: Mobile Anti-Aircraft Artillery ARMOR: [Turret] 70mm (Front Face), 44mm (Sides)
[Hull] 44mm (Front Face), 36mm (Sides), 24mm (Rear), 18mm (E. Deck)
MAIN GUN: 2 x 40x300mm Alecto MACHINE GUNS: None AMMUNITION: 120 40mm Rounds
OVERALL LENGTH: 7.82 meters/25.65 feet OVERALL HEIGHT: 3.54 meters/11.6 feet ENGINES: 2 Jelcz FSB-1 w. 2 V-12 Fractal Petroleum
POWER TO WEIGHT RATIO: 13.4 kW/t | 18.3 hp/t MAXIMUM SPEED: 67.6kph/42mph (On Road) TRAVEL RANGE: 180 km
Mobile AAA for armored formations became very valuable as mechanized warfare met the emergence of powerful, fast strike aircraft, and moreso when helicopters became viable for combat use. The dual 40mm guns have a lower rate of fire (90 rounds per minute each) than the RARDEM gun, but are paired, and have a higher elevation capability. The Siracusa has no close combat machine gun, and must be protected by infantry.
