Chapter 142: Vulpesylvanian Campaign VI


Part 1: Argeios Offensive - Opening Move

TIME: 07:34 EET
DATE: War Day 1063/1 Tertious, 1005 C.S.
LOCATION: Front line area, Liveria, Western Argeios, Principality of Vulpesylvania


The front line advanced with the sound of an officer's whistle. Armored vehicles, backed by infantry, started rolling forward, with a rolling barrage of mechanized artillery firing far ahead of them, to decimate the second line of defense.

I was seated in the main commander's cupola of the Ratte tank loaned to me by Major General Stahl. I watched through binoculars as our allies advanced sharply. NEIGHTO was taking over the main war in Vulpesylvania. While these troops were mostly green, they were trained by Veterans.

Applejack was in the tank with me. There was lots of room, too. Immediately with me would be Broken Dawn, Golden Wattle, Fluttershy, Dusk Seeker, Rarity Seeker, and Trixie Lulamoon. Other members of CAG were interspersed with both NEIGHTO allies and our own Equestrian forces. We had A LOT of combat experience available, and the survivors of this battle would be blooded veterans themselves, taught by the best.

Dusk hopped up on the back of the tank turret, and slid over to the cupola from behind.

Dusk: "Looking good. We should be able to push them all the way back to Ptolemais." He nodded.

K: "We're dropping Webley's 1st Airborne Brigade back there, along with the Ambleonian 1st Parachute Brigade, and the German 1st Fallschrimjaeger Brigade. That's a whole Division locking the back door. They'll have a route out through Ermankla to retreat across the border." I leaned on the gun mount. "Combined with our offensives at Zoni and Paliuri, this should completely throw them out of the country."

Dusk: "So we hope." He nodded.

K: "After that, we evaluate and make sure the Vulpes are fully geared." I shrugged.

Dusk: "Then the... volunteers... help them liberate a bit more." He nodded with a grin.

K: "Mhm. I've put some thought into the supporting Rainbow Factory operation." I looked at him. "I want the Elements there. And I want you leading it." I nodded.

Dusk: "I wasn't around for the last one - Webley was." He shrugged a bit.

K: "Yeah, and.. she.. had some serious issues when she got in there." I nodded, as the artillery started to roll further away, signalling that the first line was through their first wave of objectives.

Dusk: "And if Tirek shows up?" He shrugged.

K: "Me being there isn't going to make that much difference. Besides, I don't think he will be." I rubbed my chin with my left hand. "Call it a hunch. These are all his allies, not his own troops. There'll be some Ympha scientists, but not him."

Dusk: "I hope you're right." He shrugged. "What'd'we do after this is over?"

K: "Go home for a while, go over all the books and things we got from Ambleon that we never got time to read through. Rest and replenish." I nodded. "Later this year, probably Summer, we'll try to find the Rainbow of Light again."

K: "Yep." I smiled a bit. "I'm looking forward to ending this war." I keyed up my mic. C> "Alpha Actual to Line Bravo. Prepare to advance!"

Dusk: "Really, K.." He smiled. Warmly. "I owe you a lot. I want you to come by.. at least once a week. Something." He shrugged.

K: "You taking odds I'll be allowed to stay here?" I looked over at him.

Dusk: "I want you to." He nodded. "You know we all do."

K: "I'd like nothing more." I smiled back. C> "Line Bravo - ADVANCE!" I nodded to him. "Get to your jeep."

Dusk scurried off, and the massive tank lumbered forwards.. slowly. It wasn't built for speed. I sat at the cupola, watching, as we pressed onwards. The advance of the second line was intentionally slower than the first. Both to sweep out any possible leftovers, and to provide a solid wall for the first line to withdraw behind, if something came up.

I sat and watched. For once, everything was going according to plan. And I knew, from experience, this usually meant trouble. We'd had plenty of it so far. More than our share, to be sure. Having to do that whole Power Rangers bit to handle YET ANOTHER ancient enemy, for one. Dealing with the Greek Gods. The Darkness Wizard that the Macedonks sent. It was a.. to be fair.. eventful fight.

K: C> "Alpha Actual to Pluton Acutual." Prance's unit commander's call sign. "Status Report."

GdB Grande Bravado: C> "Oui, this is Pluton." A pause. "We have taken, thusfar, 7% casualties, and suffered some loss of vehicle support. Zee enemy is dug in, mon ami, and fights with ferocity. However, we are zee superior force, un' have uprooted them at every turn. Status on Line Bravo?"

TIME: 09:19 EET
DATE: War Day 1063/1 Tertious, 1005 C.S.
LOCATION: Ptolemais, Western Argeios, Principality of Vulpesylvania


I stepped forwards, with Applejack, Golden Wattle, Broken Dawn, and the power of a Ratte tank covering me. I held up a white flag, high over my head. I'd rather talk about this. The Macedonkian unit - identified as the 301st and 507th Infantry Regiments - was already damaged and under strength. While they held the town, they were surrounded. I wanted them to simply surrender.

K: "I AM GENERAL K OF EQUESTRIA!" I shouted as I got close, seeing no guards out front. "I WISH TO SPEAK TO THE COMMANDER IN CHARGE! WE OFFER TERMS OF SURRENDER!"

There was silence. Nothing. Moreover, I was unarmed, as was the party.

Applejack: "Ah gots a bad feelin' - lahk, somethin' big's wrong here. Very wrong." She frowned.

Golden: "Aye, ah' had enough experience 'till now to know, she's right." He frowned a bit.

Right about then, a dark, foreboding wind gently howled across our faces. All the ponies immediately jumped, grew stiff with unreasoned fear, and when they landed, cowered BEHIND MY LEGS, chattering their teeth, and making small, terrified whinnies.

ME? Well, being both totally vulnerable to magic, AND a 'dead head' as my (human) wife calls me, such a thing felt more like a stinky skunk walked in front of me. That is to say, I was reviled, but not scared.

Still, seeing three hardened combat veterans - Applejack especially - get scared like that? I backed up. Fast.

They needed ZERO ENCOURAGEMENT to follow suit. Once we were clear, they all ran behind the Ratte and rearmed as fast as they could.

K: "Something's up here." I nodded, and grabbed for the radio pack in Fluttershy's jeep. C> "Alpha Actual to all callsigns - Code Lavender. Elements assemble at my position. Ranger One Actual and Skyfall Actual, move to my position and set up temporary field HQ. All other callsigns on full alert."

Code Lavender was one step before Code Teal. Teal was Tirek. Lavender was a general "there is magical fuckery about, and magic or the Elements of Harmony are needed to defeat it."

Applejack: "H-Honeybun..!" She clung to me. "A-Ah dunno what that was! But, whatever it was, t'ain't nat-cher-all!" She was STILL quivering.

Golden: "A-aye, suh!" He was trying to be brave, too, but failing.

Broken: "K-K.. th-this is just a-as bad a-as th-the castle!" He was practically hugging the Ratte, clinging to his Grant like it was a mere pointy stick.

K: "Fluttershy?" I nodded to her.

Fluttershy: "..I guess I wasn't close enough to feel it. I'm sorry." She shrugged her wings, and gestured for Golden to come over for a hug. "There, there."

Golden: "A'hm.. sorry.. it's just so scary.." He shuddered. "An' I don't even know why!"

K: "Whatever it is, it doesn't make me afraid, just disgusted." I shrugged.

Applejack: "Skurr'd the apples outta me, fer shure!" She was clinging to me - VERY unlike her.

Broken: "I-I'm having the same problem." He was trying VERY hard to keep it together.

K: "We'll get answers." I nodded.


TIME: 12:33 EET


Our retaking of the town was delayed, so the Reserve Force, made up of the Navarese 1st Division, overtook us to take our place. Equestrian forces were centered on the town. Most of the 2nd line was here. Overall, the offensive had completed the rest of its objectives. We heard from the offensives to the west that the enemy broke and ran at the first sign of armored units. The border was guarded by 3 Tarpangoslavian Mechanized Divisions with tank support. An uneasy ceasefire was in place in those two locations.

I had ordered, under my authority as Supreme Allied Commander, that if the Tarpangoslavians attacked first, NEIGHTO forces could advance and destroy them.. and hold whatever territory they took. I was not going to make similar mistakes as Korea and Vietnam, that is, take territory and destroy the enemy, then withdraw, and invite them to attack again. No, we would take and hold if pressed.

The Elements were all here, as was all of CAG, minus a few (Forest Spirit, Redheart, Saquet and his Legionnaires - all off to the west, commanding the offensives there). We were as together as we could get.

Part 4: Argeios Offensive - Battle of Ptolemais Pt 3


TIME: 13:34 EET
DATE: War Day 1063/1 Tertious, 1005 C.S.
LOCATION: Ptolemais, Western Argeios, Principality of Vulpesylvania


Without being told the situation, Astral Sky figured out everything that had transpired, what was going on here, the second she arrived. Moreso, she figured out who was responsible and where they are. And why.. why? Because, as we found out, Thestrals were the descendants of Flutter Ponies. That, as I surmised, Galactica Spark could not actually kill. She could not genocide. She was still a Pony, and deliberate, malicious mass murder was not something she could ever actually do. Instead, she cursed.

That was a guess.

Now, the fact, was that 4th Platoon and I, with Dusk and Twilight, were heading into towards that evil person, that was doing all of this, so we could attack the source of the missing foxes. And once they were dealt with, we could free them.

It was empty almost the whole way to the city's administration building. Out front were actual Tirek-uniformed troops - Raptorians.

Astral: "Squad and change." She nodded, and motioned for 2nd Squad to move up - they had suppressed weapons. After a second, the Raptorians were dead.

K: "Move in." I nodded, and raised my rifle. "Buffalo the mage, kill them fast." I said as we got to the door.

Astral and 1st Squad kicked the door, hucked in two grenades, and shut the door again, letting the fragmentation grenades do their work. They then popped the door open, and rushed in at full speed. I took position between 1st and 2nd Squad.

Inside, we took fire from the balcony - more Raptorians. I was barely able to get off a single shot before 1st Squad had them dealt with and dead.

Elites indeed.

Astral: "..they're close. Very close." She murmured.
