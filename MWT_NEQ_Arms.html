My Little Pony: Marking Warfare's Tale

Kingdom of New Equestria: Firearms



Weapon and equipment acquisition in New Eqeustria is fitful at best. Small batches made over long numbers of years means gear isn't totally standardized down to the fraction of the millimeter, just generally compatible and cross-functional. Often, there has not been enough money or resources to do force-wide changes, and certain units have received incremental upgrades where others have not. Weapons may be brand new, or they may be 10-50 years old or more. They do all work, though, and weapons from the same series are guaranteed to have parts compatibility. Springfield Arsenal in Maresechusetts is the only remaining major military manufacturer, though Maretime Bay does have a small weapons production capability on par with the Manehattan Special Warfare Center from a thousand years ago.


M-47 (series) Roc
WEAPON TYPE: Handgun
MANUFACTURERS: Springfield Arsenal
CALIBER: 10x23mm
CAPACITY: 14 + 1 Round Detachable Box Magazine
EFFECTIVE RANGE: 60 meters
BARREL LENGTH: 120mm/4.72 inches (standard)
OVERALL LENGTH: 222mm/8.74 inches
OVERALL HEIGHT: 154mm/6.1 inches
TOTAL MASS: 3.2lbs/1.45kg
The M-47 Roc comes in different finishes, with different hammer configurations from DAO to DA/SA to Single Action, with or without extended, threaded barrels, and with or without dust cover rail systems. It is the standard sidearm of the New Equestrian Armed Forces. The standard round is a 9.75gm (150gr) flatnose full metal jacket round, traveling at 400 meters per second, generating 780 joules of muzzle energy. This is roughly analogous to the .400 Corbon round from Earth. This is a HUGE jump from the older Morgan it was based on, which gave 580j from the muzzle, and on par with the old Sub-Machine Guns. It also has a 135gr/8.75gm armor piercing round with a velocity of 440 meters per second, and has 850 joules of muzzle energy. It has an effective range of 60 meters with iron sights, but this can be extended with the use of optical sights, which some Roc pistols have rails for, on top of the slide. While not fully automatic, and lacking a stock, it does have the potential range of a sub-machine gun or personal defense weapon.
M-47A9 Black Roc SOP
WEAPON TYPE: Handgun
MANUFACTURERS: Springfield Arsenal (Canterlogic Industries)
CALIBER: 10x23mm
CAPACITY: 14 + 1 Round Detachable Box Magazine
EFFECTIVE RANGE: 60 meters
BARREL LENGTH: 132mm/5.2 inches (standard)
OVERALL LENGTH: 234mm/9.12 inches
OVERALL HEIGHT: 154mm/6.1 inches
TOTAL MASS: 3.2lbs/1.45kg
Made at Canterlogic Industries, under the watchful eye of Lieutenant General Phyllis Cloverleaf, the Black Roc is designed for Special Operations use, and is issued to the only remaining Ranger Regiment in the New Equestrian Army. The Black Roc is a slicked up Roc pistol, with a threaded, extended barrel with a rubber O-Ring for even better seal against escaping gas. The weapon itself is the best example of the Roc series in New Equestria's arsenal. However, only 372 have been made. The Black Roc is intended to use a special loading with its suppressor, the 200gr (13gm) bullet, travelling at a modest 215 meters-per-second, for a shallow 300 joules of muzzle energy - however, the shot is almost totally silent, with the sound of the slide moving back and forth being louder than the shot's report.
M-51A3 Manticore
WEAPON TYPE: Pulse Rifle, Caseless
MANUFACTURERS: Springfield Arsenal
CALIBER: 6x40mm Caseless
CAPACITY: 60 Round Detachable Box Magazine
EFFECTIVE RANGE: 600 meters (computerized sights)
BARREL LENGTH: 532mm/21 inches
OVERALL LENGTH: 856mm/33.7 inches
OVERALL HEIGHT: 200mm/7.87 inches
TOTAL MASS: 9.2lbs/4.1kg
The Manticore Pulse Rifle was first fielded in 2004 C.S., and has undergone minor modifications and batch changes since then. A pattern of rifle rather than a single mass issued batch of clones, the figures are approximate, as different batches in different amounts have been made over the last 50 years. The weapon uses computerized sights, as well as analogue optics. It is the primary weapon system of most of the NERA (New Equestria Royal Army), NERN (Navy), NERAF (Air Force), and so on. The 6mm caseless round develops over 2,600 joules of energy at the muzzle, and is accurate within 2MOA with non-computerized sights. It accepts a wide variety of attachments and under-barrel weapon systems. It is called a "Pulse Rifle" as it fires by electrical impulse, not traditional striker or hammer fire on a brass casing and primer. The rounds have absolutely no casing or seperated primer pocket. The propellant and primer are part of one solid block of materal.
M-52A3 Manticore Carbine
WEAPON TYPE: Pulse Rifle (Carbine), Caseless
MANUFACTURERS: Springfield Arsenal
CALIBER: 6x40mm Caseless
CAPACITY: 60 Round Detachable Box Magazine
EFFECTIVE RANGE: 600 meters (computerized sights)
BARREL LENGTH: 406mm/16 inches
OVERALL LENGTH: 728mm/28.6 inches
OVERALL HEIGHT: 200mm/7.87 inches
TOTAL MASS: 8.2lbs/3.7kg
The Manticore Carbine takes the doctrinal place of Sub-Machine Guns and Personal Defense Weapons in the New Equestrian Army arsenal. It's shorter, and easier to carry and use. It has the same effective range as the full rifle, with computerized sights. However, it is less precise. The 21-inch barrel of the full rifle is also used by Designated Marksponies. The Carbine variant is used mainly by Gendarmes and second line troops, except in Grenadier and under barrel weapon configurations.
M-53A1 Kelpie
WEAPON TYPE: Shotgun Attachmment
MANUFACTURERS: Springfield Arsenal
CALIBER: 12 Gauge, 2.75-inch (70mm)
CAPACITY: 2 + 1 Round Detachable Tube Magazine
EFFECTIVE RANGE: 35 meters (Shot) - 60 meters (Slugs)
BARREL LENGTH: 190mm/7.5 inches
OVERALL LENGTH: 435mm/17.1 inches
OVERALL HEIGHT: 80mm/3.2 inches
TOTAL MASS: 4.4lbs/2kg
A simple shotgun based on inertia action, the Kelpie is designed to be attached to the Manticore rifle, and is part of the Manticore Weapon System Attachment family. It only holds three shells total, and is intended to be primarily used with FRAG-12 shells. The FRAG-12 shell is a micro grenade designed to blow apart small to medium sized shadow creatures, and either defeat them or drive them away. Its other primary function is to carry Door Breacher shells, and be used to force entry into a structure without forcing the pointpony or breacher to transition back and forth between a breaching shotgun and their main rifle.
M-54A2 Hydra
WEAPON TYPE: Grenade Launcher
MANUFACTURERS: Springfield Arsenal
CALIBER: 40x53mm Grenade
CAPACITY: 1 Round Chamber
EFFECTIVE RANGE: 600 meters (point target), 800 meters (area target)
BARREL LENGTH: 160mm/6.3 inches
OVERALL LENGTH: 384mm/15.1 inches
OVERALL HEIGHT: 92mm/3.62 inches
TOTAL MASS: 2.2lbs/1kg
Dark Creatures, Darklies, Shadow Mmonsters and whatever other name they go by, can only be defeated by magic. "Elemental" grenades - Napalm, Liquid Nitrogen, and Acid - can be used to ward them off. As anything like that, will always have a magical element to it no matter what, even with Magic sealed, they can harm the dark monsters. Enough to drive them away. Computerized grenadier sights are available for them, but are uncommon. The Hydra is essentially the thousand-year-old Gavin grenade launcher, with very slight alterations.
M-55A1 Flashbee
WEAPON TYPE: Electric Projector ("Taser")
MANUFACTURERS: Springfield Arsenal (Canterlogic Industries)
CALIBER: N/A
CAPACITY: 30 Shocks at Full Charge/Full Power
EFFECTIVE RANGE: 10 meters
BARREL LENGTH: N/A
OVERALL LENGTH: 435mm/17.1 inches
OVERALL HEIGHT: 154mm/6.1 inches
TOTAL MASS: 4.4lbs/2kg
One of the more interesting attachments for the Manticore is the Flashbee. It fires a small electrically-condutcive chemical jet with a range of 10 meters, then passes a current through it, of 1000v at 2 Amps. This is enough to cause immediate electrical stunning of large animals, and carries a threat of lethal force if it hits the heart directly. A half power setting is also available for less-than-lethal stunning of large to medium sized creatures, or lethal stunning to small ones. Again, as Magic has been 'sealed' for over 1,000 years, alternative means to drive off Shadow Creatures has been required, and the half power setting is enough to generally drive away the larger Shadow Creatures. As with everything in this world, despite magic being 'sealed' this does carry a small magical element to it, sufficient enough to proudce its warding effect. As a side benefit, the half power setting is also sufficient to stun a Pony should they need to be restrained. The North Atlantic Commmand's Gendarmes (Maresechuseets, Rowed Island, Cobnecticut) are the primary users.
