Part 1: Thick, Sticky Layers

TIME: 08:53 ERST
DATE: War Day 877/1 Noneus, 1004 C.S.
LOCATION: Royal Palace, Canterlot

After a week, I was intentionally moved to Canterlot both for my own safety, and so I could recover basking in the magical aura of both Princesses. I had to admit, it actually did help. Applejack and my kids were all here, too. Ponyville was on extra alert, trying to find out how that assassination team got so close.

Despite the magi-injury, and the aches (which were getting lighter by the day), I still had physical energy. I needed to get up and walk. Dr. Whooves told me this was natural, that because of the "magi-damage" to my body, my muscles would feel itchy and restless until I was fully healed. He warned me, given the level of injury I sustained, this could take a long time.

I walked into the kitchen, wanting to make breakfast myself, despite Hekkon, the Griffin Chef, wanting to practically nurse-maid me with his (admittedly excellent) food.

What I found was Celestia.. stuffing cake into her mouth.

K: "Cel?" I cocked a brow, and approached.

Celestia: "Eh!?" She was taken completely by surprise.. and I realized she'd been eating like a savage - there was cake all over her mouth, and she was stuffing it into her pie hole with both hooves. "Oh! K!" She hid her face with her wings. "Pl.. please.. don't.. don't look at me!"

I could see the teardrops hit the floor.

I immediately moved over to her, slid to my knees, and held her closely to me.

K: "They're alive, Cel." I knew what she was feeling.. I just.. knew. "We're alive. We made it. The dead are dead, but the living are living. And because we had all the right things, we've saved so many lives.." I stroked her (flat, horsehair, NOT astral) mane.

She just sobbed, and kept stuffing.

Celestia: "Don't.. deserve.. I should be FAT and DISGUSTING! On the OUTSIDE like.."

I grabbed her forelegs, and forced them away from the cake.

K: "No. Don't." I shook my head. "Look at me, Cel." I pulled her towards me.

Celestia: "I WAS HORRIBLE! I was a MONSTER inside!" She shrieked. "I.. I was so scared.. you'd find out the truth.. that.. that you'd kill me.. for it." She couldn't bring herself to hug me. "I should look like I was.. I don't.. deserve.."

K: "Cel.. I know.. I know I knew the truth. I just didn't care. Honest? From what little I remember, I may have seen you as better than Tirek. But.. I.. don't think I trusted you." I grabbed her muzzle, and made her look me in the eyes. "But, that was not ME-me. This me. The real me. *I* trust you." I forced her to hug me. "I trust you, and I care for you in a way I can't put into words. You called yourself their mom. I'm Dad. I know it, I feel it, with every order I issue.. we aren't married, but we ARE their parents." I pulled her in tightly. "You. And me. Us. Not whoever those two were."

She just cried. And cried.

I was amazed she held it in as long as she had. She remembered EVERYTHING about the other timeline. Everything. And she saw the despot she'd become.. her worst fear come to life.. and it reviled, repulsed, and disgusted her. She was absolutely, completely, disgusted with her own self.

So.. she ate. She ate something she felt would make her fat and disgusting on the outside, the way she felt about herself on the inside.

I had stopped her.

And she had cried.
