My Little Pony: Warfare is Magic

Crystal Kingdom: Airships (Aerofleet of the Crystal Kingdom)



Equestria, initially, did not have an armed Navy. The Royal Guard maintained a small fleet of small sailing vessels for search and rescue operations, and the Royal Yacht, a Schooner-sized vessel, for pleasure excursions. Constructing a fighting navy from the ground up, General K had to initially adapt to the shipbuilding capability of the kingdom as it existed. After the 2nd Attack on Canterlot, however, the Steel Fleet Program was pushed forwards, despite the technical limitations of existing shipbuilding industry. While steel ships could be made from the very beginning, they had no concept of how ship armor worked at the time, and could not have constructed a warship. Hence, an initial reduction in technological era to facilitate faster shipbuilding and allow for the warships themselves to be self-repairable in the field, instead of outright armored to withstand attacks.
Equestria has several major naval facilities, but the Navy itself is headquarted at Fillydephia Naval Yards, in Docksylvania. Other facilities include Baleston Naval Yards, Manehattan Naval Shipyards, and New Haven Naval Base. The Port of Mayaimi also has a dedicated set of docks for the Equestrian Navy that later became Saletine Naval Base.
Equestria uses the prefix "HMS" (Her Majesty's Ship) for its vessels. As Equestria is afforded the leading place in North Equus, all other nations with Royal governments use a prefix variant denoting their nation.

The XY-series vessels remaining in the time-frozen capital of the Crystal Kingdom for 1,000 years are none the worse for wear, though they required the installation of weapons, as they were never meant to be used in combat. Instead, they were pleasure vessels, or transportation for Officials to other cities within the Kingdom. Reconditioned, they have the same effective role as Air Warships as a Patrol Gunboat - extremely light armament, and designed for scouting or to cover larger vessels while in harbor, or as close protection. They're also used as landing and special operations transport craft. With enough room to hold up to 32 troops, the four remaining vessels, between them, can transport two whole companies of Crystal Kingdom Aero Marines, AND get them to an altitude where they can perform parachute drops.
