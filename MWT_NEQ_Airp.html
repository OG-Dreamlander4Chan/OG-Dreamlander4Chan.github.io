My Little Pony: Marking Warfare's Tale

Kingdom of New Equestria: Aircraft



New Equestria's aircraft have areas of operation primarily focusing on land warfare. While New Equestria has a Navy with an LHVA, the only aircraft they have that functions on it are helicopters. It was intended that Equestria acquire a V/STOVL aircraft to equip the LHVA's, but the ability of two different helicopters to fire anti-ship missiles was seen as sufficient.


RAH-14 Banshee
DESIGNATION: Recon/Attack Helicopter 14 "Banshee" ROLE: Multirole, Reconnaisence and Attack AIRCRAFT TYPE: Rotary Wing
GUN ARMAMENT: M27 20mm 3-barrel rotary cannon ORDNANCE ARMAMENT: M72 Wing Armament SubSystem. Internal Hardpoints mount AGM-44 Bronco & AIM-48 Bugbear HARD POINTS: 4 with M72 WASS. 4 Internal for AGM-44, 2 Internal for AIM-48.
OVERALL LENGTH: 14.44 meters/47.4 feet OVERALL HEIGHT: (Mast Radar) 4 meters/13.1 feet ENGINES: 2 Whycoming LRV-34, 1600shp
CRUISE SPEED: 165 knots MAXIMUM SPEED: 175 knots/TD> FERRY RANGE: 1200 nmi with conformal fuel tanks
New Equestria's Swiss Army Knife platform, the RAH-66 is intended to perform the Attack Helicopter role, as well as a Recon role. Without the wing subsystems attached, it has a tiny radar signature, making it a very nimble craft as well. With the M72 WSS attached, it can hit ships, tanks, light vehicles, and infantry in the open. The 20mm rotary cannon can even be folded back to cut the radar signature even more.
OH-17 Sidhe
DESIGNATION: Observation Helicopter 17 "Sidhe" ROLE: Multirole, Light Strike and Air Cavalry Scout AIRCRAFT TYPE: Compound Rotary Wing with Pusher
GUN ARMAMENT: M8 11mm cannon ORDNANCE ARMAMENT: M71 Wing Armament SubSystem HARD POINTS: 2 with M72 WASS
OVERALL LENGTH: 11.6 meters/38 feet OVERALL HEIGHT: 3.52 meters/11.5 feet ENGINES: 2 Whycoming LRV-34, 1600shp
CRUISE SPEED: 170 knots MAXIMUM SPEED: 185 knots/TD> FERRY RANGE: 1250 nmi with conformal fuel tanks
Designed for multiple uses, the OH-17 can perform the functions of an Air Cavalry Scout platform with 1 Squad of loaded combat troops, some light rockets, and a single 11mm machine gun carrying 1,120 rounds firing at 1120rpm, or as a team transport helicopter, unarmed, and with conformal fuel tanks. Its amazing 1250 nmi range is technically far more than New Equestria would ever need, but it was intended from the start to also be used as a naval asset, to transport insertion teams from the LHVA. While, doctrinally, this was never practiced, the ability was there. A special VIP version was created as an "air chariot" for Princess Theia.
