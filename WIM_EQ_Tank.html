My Little Pony: Warfare is Magic

Kingdom of Equestria: Ground Vehicles



Motorized carriages were, at best, a novelty when the war began. Engine technology was usually some form of shrunk-down steam engine. After the rescue of the Crystal Kingdom, and Bright Eyes, miniaturization of Fractal Petroleum (Diesel) engines became possible, leading to the development of tanks. Tirek had already done so, somehow, though his armored vehicles were powered by Petroleum (Petrol/Gas). The fielding of "Tirek Heavy Tanks" at the same time as the first engagement of the M-1 led, eventually, to the discontinuation of "Tank Classes" and the development of the Main Battle Tank.


M-1 Tassigny

MAIN GUN: 76x320mm Gun MACHINE GUN: 2 x 11mm EM-4 Browning (1 Pintile, 1 Coax) AMMUNITION: 70 76mm Shells, 1200 11mm Rounds
OVERALL LENGTH: 7.6 meters/25 feet OVERALL HEIGHT: 3.44 meters/11.3 feet ENGINES: 2 Continental AGE-1 V-12 Fractal Petroleum
POWER TO WEIGHT RATIO: 11.5kw/tonne | 13.7hp/t MAXIMUM SPEED: 57kph/35mph (On Road) TRAVEL RANGE: 100 km
Equestria's first tank, the Tassigny was an experiment in how to create an armored vehicle fit for ponies. It worked in its job well enough, but the 76mm gun was found to be underpowered against Tirek Heavy Tanks, and the armor somewhat thin compared to their 150mm short-barrel howitzers. As such, Tassigny's were soon re-armed with the more powerful 77mm Tank Gun (76.2x560mm).
