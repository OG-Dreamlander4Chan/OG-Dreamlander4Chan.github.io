Roaman Empire: Warships (Marina del Regno)



Roam is very proud of their navy, and while not nearly as disciplined as the Germane fleet, their ships were always large, visually impressive, and even beautiful in their own way. A great deal of ceremony, pomp-and-circumstance, and dog-and-pony shows went along with this, despite Roam losing its last major war against the former Ottoman Empire. However, this has led the rest of the European nations with a bad impression of Roam's military - that of a "force of show-offs" instead of a "show of force." This reputation was quickly buried during the Neighpon Liberation Campaign.. unfortunately, along side the graves of many brave Roaman Legionis.
Roam uses the ship prefix "RNS" for Roaman Naval Ship. They do not use a hull classification system at all, and instead, ships are assigned Dock Numbers that are only ever used on internal reports, and not as an official part of a ship's designation (Equestria, by comparison, would list a ship like "HMS Canterlot, CA-01").


Imperio-Class
CLASS NAME: Imperio (Ceasar) SHIPS IN CLASS: Caesar, Rex
SHIP TYPE: Battlecruiser ENGINES: 24 Ceirano Water Tube Boilers, 8 IVECO-Ferrari Geared Turbines SCREWS: 4 SPEED: 30kts
PRIMARY ARMAMENT: 4 x 4 255mm/L40 (10"/40) SECONDARY ARMAMENT: 3 x 2 200mm/L40 (7.9"/40) TERTIARY ARMAMENT: 10 x 1 105mm/50cal casemates (4"/50) MACHINE GUNS: 11 x 2 11mm Breda M-02
OVERALL LENGTH: 145 meters/475 feet NUMBER OF DECKS: 5 Belowdeck, 1 Superstructure, 3 Conning Tower BEAM: 27 meters/88.5 feet DRAFT: 7.7 meters/25 feet
TRANSIT RANGE: 1110 nmi TOTAL CREW: 790 Enlisted, 120 Petty Officers, 45 Officers MARINE DETACHMENT: 120 NUMBER OF BOATS: 12 x 5 meter launches
Compared to the Germane battleships, the Roaman battlecruisers are lighter, much faster (the fastest vessels in the NEIGHTO combined fleet) and both more nimble.. and prettier. A design flaw of the vessel has two of its main gun turrets amidships, instead of fore-and-aft as with the other NEIGHTO partners. While this does limit its ability to bring its full weight-of-shell against an enemy vessel, when it CAN, it has 16 total naval rifles it can fire, giving it an unexpectedly impressive broadside with an equally impressive range, for such a light caliber.
Regioni-Class
CLASS NAME: Regioni SHIPS IN CLASS: Latium, Piemonte, Venetia, Lombardia, Toscana, Umbria, Lucania, Liguria
SHIP TYPE: Protected Cruiser ENGINES: 18 Ceirano Water Tube Boilers, 8 IVECO-Ferrari Geared Turbines SCREWS: 4 SPEED: 32.5kts
PRIMARY ARMAMENT: 2 x 2 150mm/L45 (5.9"/45) SECONDARY ARMAMENT: 2 x 2 & 6 x 1 105mm/50cal (5.9"/50) TERTIARY ARMAMENT: None MACHINE GUNS: 12 x 2 11mm Breda M-02
OVERALL LENGTH: 120 meters/393 feet NUMBER OF DECKS: 5 Belowdeck, 1 Superstructure, 2 Conning Tower BEAM: 24 meters/78.7 feet DRAFT: 7.7 meters/25 feet
TRANSIT RANGE: 1120 nmi TOTAL CREW: 400 Enlisted, 90 Petty Officers, 20 Officers MARINE DETACHMENT: 120 NUMBER OF BOATS: 8 x 5 meter launches, 2 x 10 meter launch
Fast and light, the Protected Cruisers of the Roaman fleet may have a light gun, but they can fire fast, and from a decently good range. Their high speed allows them to get OUT of trouble as fast as they get INTO it, too, allowing for excellent hit-and-run strikes.
Province-Class
CLASS NAME: Province SHIPS IN CLASS: Viterbo, Rieti, Torino, Asti, Venezia, Padova, Pavia, Lodi, Firenze, Pisa, Perugia, Terni, Potenza, Matera, Savona, Genoa
SHIP TYPE: Scout Cruiser ENGINES: 14 Ceirano Water Tube Boilers, 8 IVECO-Ferrari Geared Turbines SCREWS: 4 SPEED: 35kts
PRIMARY ARMAMENT: 4 x 2 150mm/L45 (5.9"/45) SECONDARY ARMAMENT: None TERTIARY ARMAMENT: None MACHINE GUNS: 12 x 2 11mm Breda M-02
OVERALL LENGTH: 100 meters/328 feet NUMBER OF DECKS: 5 Belowdeck, 1 Superstructure, 2 Conning Tower BEAM: 20 meters/65 feet DRAFT: 6.9 meters/22.6 feet
TRANSIT RANGE: 1130 nmi TOTAL CREW: 220 Enlisted, 75 Petty Officers, 25 Officers MARINE DETACHMENT: 80 NUMBER OF BOATS: 4 x 5 meter launches, 2 x 10 meter launches
The "hot rods" of the Roaman fleet, the lightly armed Province-class Scout Cruisers have no torpedo armament, and only a light gun armament, but again, benefit from a high rate of fire. Mostly suited to its scouting role, with some capability of fighting light vessels, the Province-class ships are considered "unprotected cruisers" as they don't have armor specifically to guard its engine and magazine spaces, just the standard deck and side armor.
Comune-Class
CLASS NAME: Comune SHIPS IN CLASS: Arezzo, Altamura, Cortona, Manciano, Carzano, Cogne, Ravenna, Montello
SHIP TYPE: Fleet Destroyer ENGINES: 8 Alfa Romeo Fractal Distillate Engines SCREWS: 4 SPEED: 35.5kts
PRIMARY ARMAMENT: 4 x 2 15cm L/50 (5.9"/50) SECONDARY ARMAMENT: None TERTIARY ARMAMENT: 3 x 4 480mm R1 Oxygen Torpedo MACHINE GUNS: 10 x 2 11mm Breda M-02
OVERALL LENGTH: 97.5 meters/320 feet NUMBER OF DECKS: 4 Belowdeck, 1 Superstructure + 1 Conning Tower, 1 Flying Bridge BEAM: 12 meters/40 feet DRAFT: 4.1 meters/13.45 feet
TRANSIT RANGE: 900 nmi TOTAL CREW: 60 Enlisted, 15 Petty Officers, 10 Officers MARINE DETACHMENT: 12 NUMBER OF BOATS: 2 x 5 meter launch
Roam's primary torpedo delivery system, the Comune-class Destroyers boast an impressive 12 torpedo spread, and a very high speed, allowing them to get in close, fast, launch, and withdraw before most peer enemy vessels can track them with their guns. The 480mm R1 torpedo is very fast, at 51 knots.

SHIP TYPE: Ocean-Going Torpedo Boat ("Schnellboot") ENGINES: 4 Alfa Romeo Fractal Distillate Engines SCREWS: 4 SPEED: 38kts
PRIMARY ARMAMENT: 2 x 2 15cm L/50 (5.9"/50) SECONDARY ARMAMENT: None TERTIARY ARMAMENT: 3 x 4 480mm R1 Oxygen Torpedo MACHINE GUNS: 7 x 2 11mm Breda M-02
OVERALL LENGTH: 62 meters/203 feet NUMBER OF DECKS: 3 Belowdeck + 1 Bridge BEAM: 9 meters/29.5 feet DRAFT: 3.8 meters/12.5 feet
TRANSIT RANGE: 650 nmi TOTAL CREW: 30 Enlisted, 10 Petty Officers, 5 Officers MARINE DETACHMENT: None NUMBER OF BOATS: None
These corvettes are barely NOT PT Boats, only because their hull design was intended from the start to be ocean-going, and they have a decent range without refuelling. As with the Destroyers, they have an impressive 12 torpedo spread, and a very high speed.
