My Little Pony: Warfare is Magic

Federal Republic of Canida: Aircraft (Canidan Forces Air Force)



The CFAF benefitted from a fairly close relationship with outer Crystal Kingdom settlements, and after the restoration of the Crystal Heart, the Canidan Federal Government pushed immediately to try and create an aircraft for Canid use. As Canids have, essentially, proper digits, they can use things like a Human might, with some restrictions, and at 7/8ths scale. The CFAF has control over all flying craft in the Canidan Forces, including stationing pilots and administrative staff aboard the Fleet's Carrier.


CFA-1 Firefox
DESIGNATION: Canidan Fighter Attack-1 "Firefox" ROLE: Multirole Fighter/Strike Aircraft AIRCRAFT TYPE: Lifting Body with limited neutral buoyancy
GUN ARMAMENT: 2 x 1 11mm EM-4 Browning ORDNANCE ARMAMENT: Up to 750lbs of explosives HARD POINTS: 4 internal
OVERALL LENGTH: 7.8 meters/25.6 feet OVERALL HEIGHT: 2.8 meters/9.18 feet ENGINES: 1 AirTech Canida ATC-F-1 Fractal Petroleum
CRUISE SPEED: 64.8kts/120kph/74.5mph MAXIMUM SPEED: 134kts/250kph/155mph FERRY RANGE: 600 nmi
Unlike the Equestrian F/A-1, the CFA-1 is much heavier, and relies more on its Lifting Body design and less on its Gas Bags to maintain altitude. If a total loss of power occured, however, even at maximum weight, the aircraft could "float" to the ground as long as its gas bags weren't damaged, and at a low enough velocity to prevent injury to the pilot. This is assuming no air currents are available to "glide" the aircraft towards the ground. As with the F/A-1, a small Jump Seat is behind the pilot that could comfortably carry a Pony, or carry a Canid or smaller Griffon with some tightness - General K, of course, would be quite cramped, but can fit.
CS-1 Hound
DESIGNATION: Canidan Scout-1 "Hound" ROLE: Multirole Scout/Anti-Sub Aircraft AIRCRAFT TYPE: Lifting Body with neutral buoyancy
GUN ARMAMENT: 2 x 1 11mm EM-4 Browning ORDNANCE ARMAMENT: Up to 500lbs/225kg of explosives HARD POINTS: 1 internal, 2 external under-wing root
OVERALL LENGTH: 9.04 meters/29.65 feet OVERALL HEIGHT: 3.24 meters/10.62 feet ENGINES: 1 AirTech Canida ATC-F-1 Fractal Petroleum
CRUISE SPEED: 64.8kts/120kph/74.5mph MAXIMUM SPEED: 134kts/250kph/155mph FERRY RANGE: 600 nmi
The CS-1 Hound is an interesting multi-role aircraft. It can carry up to three passengers, and the rear half of the cockpit slides back, while the front slides forwards, allowing those three passengers to egress from the aircraft while in flight. Instead of passengers, it can carry five 100lbs (45kg) bombs (one under each wing root, two in the internal bay), or one Depth Charge, the Canidan Forces the first to develop the Depth Charge concept in preparation for Tirek or another nation's possible discovery and use of submarines.
