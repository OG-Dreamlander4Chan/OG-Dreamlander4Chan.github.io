My Little Pony: Marking Warfare's Tale



Chapter 4: Unity

Part 1: Born

TIME: 05:51 NERST
DATE: Redemption Day 10/16 Septimus, 2054 C.S.
LOCATION: Zephyr Heights, Greenbroke, New Equestria


Theia: "DADDY!"

I heard that as I stirred from my slumber. What I got was an ALICORN BODY TACKLING ME IN MY BED!

Theia: "DADDYDADDYDADDY!" She hugged me so tight, I couldn't breathe.

K: "AIR!" I gasped out. "Daddy! NEED! AIR!"

Sunny, Izzy, and Hitch all woke up, too. We'd been quartered in the same room.

They saw a dark purple Alicorn, looking a lot like Princess Luna once had, but with a different Cutie Mark, and moreover, auburn hair instead of nightly blue. She was quite large, too. My little girl was a bit bigger than Celestia had been.

She cried, I cried - sobbed - as we held each other. The others.. ehh.. just kinda shrugged and watched.

It took us a full ten minutes of blubbering over each other for us to calm down.

Theia: "Daddy..." She wiped her tears, as I wiped the snot from her nose.

K: "My baby girl." I sniffled.

Theia: "Oh, Daddy.. I'm.. I'm so happy!" She started crying again. "Oh, it's been SO LONG!" And hugged me.

A thousand years for her.

Ten years for me.

K: "Mommy's gone.. honey. I'm so sorry, you never got to meet her." That got ME crying again, over J--. "I miss her.. and I missed you so much."

Theia: "Did.. you remember.. us?" She tilted her head, nuzzling me.

K: "Kinda? Like, when I slept, I dreamt of my time here. I could remember after I woke up, but.." I shook my head. "It's not like I had some group of friends that would believe me."

(Yeah, shout-out to my Discord community - what, you think the sidebars stopped with Book 1? Hell no.)

Theia: "Oh, Daddy." She finally collected herself. "As Princess of New Equestria, I restore your rank as Commander-in-Chief, General of the New Equestrian Armed Forces." She sighed. "I can do.. just this." She focused her horn intensely, at the same level as one casting a very high level spell, but all that happened, was three buttons with her Cutie Mark on them, appeared in my hand.

K: "You're.. so weak, honey." I shrugged. "But, thank you." I nodded. "I'll get these on my uniform." I kissed her snout. "Oh, shit, where are my manners?" I pointed to Sunny. "She's from Liberty."

Theia: "Greetings! Please, call me Aunt Theia. We are family." She said, her voice normalizing after the outburst, and I realized she sounded A LOT like Luna did, but with a softer voice, reminding me more of Dusk's other daughters - and if Dusk was right, that tone came from his mom.

Sunny: "Sunrise Magic Starscout. Call me Sunny." She nodded to Hitch and Izzy. "These are my friends, Hitch and Izzy."

Hitch: "Ah, your-your highness." He tried to bow, sitting up in bed.

Izzy: "Hee-hee - HIYA!" She waved dramatically.

Theia: "Oh, Daddy. So much has happened." She sighed.

Zipp: "My Goddess.." She paced in, still in her PJ's, wiping her eyes, with Pipp in tow.. "..what's going ON, K? It's so early!"

Pipp: "OHMIGOSH! PRINCESS THEIA!" She held her hooves over her mouth momentarily, then smacked Zipp a couple times, to get her to bow or something.

Theia: "Please, please, Zipp, Pipp.." She shook her head. "..your mother has already told me you're joining my daddy. No formalities."

Zipp: "Suits me fine." She hopped up on the bed Sunny was on, and lolled her head against her. "So sleepy."

Pipp: "Ohhhh... I soooooo want to stream this." She huffed.

K: "This morning, I'm making a formal announcement - the tailor should be bringing me my new Class A Formals. Once I can be properly dressed." I wagged a finger. "Theia? Daughter? I want a full report. No secrets."

Theia: "..yes, Daddy.." She looked sullen. "It all started thirty six years to the day after you beat Tirek. Most of our best veterans were elderly. The new ones were good, but not experienced. That wasn't the issue, though. Like Uncle Sombra had been, we were attacked by The Umbrals."

Sunny: "U-Umbrals?!" She stammered.

Theia: "Please, don't interrupt, this is very.. painful for me to remember." She shook her head. "Everyone at the palace scrambled for some kind of a solution. But they were everywhere. Killing so many. Dragging some back through these portals." She sniffled. "Finally, Mom and everyone.. decided the only thing they could do was seal the magic. The Umbrals wanted it. They wanted the source of all Equestrian magic, it was the only thing that could hurt them, besides the Crystal Heart. I don't know.. what happened to Aunt Cadence." She shook her head. "So, Twilight and Sunset devised a spell to infuse the Unity Crystal with all the magic in the land, wiping the slate completely clean of all magic and magical effects. They focused the energy into the largest Umbral portal, and sealed it, but in so doing.. the Unity Crystal split.. and.." She sniffled. "..I was being evacuated to Springfield when I saw the magical detonation at Canterlot. It was so huge, my helicopter crashed, and I was badly injured. Another Alicorn named Opaline Arcana found me. She was a cousin to Celestia and Luna.. through Galactica Spark's sister, she said." She shrugged. "Opaline rescued me, and now that magic was gone, she agreed to help me rebuild what was left. She's been my mentor ever since." She looked at me straight. "No one of your friends was there. The few with me.. died." She lowered her head. "Aunt Celestia and Mom designated me as the survivor of the Royal Family. They told me.. I had to be strong. Like you. And Other Daddy." She smiled a bit.

Hitch: "Whoa.. so THAT'S what happened!" He shook his head.

Theia: "Yes." She sighed, letting out a long breath. "It took us over two hundred years to even get half way back to where we once were, another hundred to stabilize what settlements we had. So many very smart ponies were lost.." She looked down and away. "..education seems pointless when you have starving foals." And sniffled sadly, only for me to hug her tightly. "After three hundred years, things became stable, and we started to improve. The old state capital building in Providence is where Parliament usually meets, though we.." She shrugged. "..we keep most of the offices here in Zephyr Heights."

K: "Yeah, we took a tram to get up here from the base of the mountain." I shrugged. "It doesn't seem to get much use."

Theia: "Yes, the tram station was once very popular, and travel between Zephyr Heights Base Station and other towns, very regular, but over the last century or so.. things have.." She looked me in the eyes. "..friendship is dying, Daddy, and I don't know how to fix it!" She started crying AGAIN, this time feeling like she'd failed me. I just hugged her.

Hitch: "This is why everything's so split up, isn't it?" He tilted his head.

Theia: "Yes. With the bonds of friendship growing lean, travel has decreased significantly." She sighed slowly. "Trade, too. You're Sheriff of Maretime Bay. Can you say that many travel outside of your Area Command?" She tilted her head.

Hitch: "..come to think of it, no. And we tend to get real jealous if some other Area Command gets up in our business." He looked at Zipp, who was half asleep. "Sorry, Zipp."

Zipp: "Mhmhh." She gave a dismissive hoof-wave. "It's all goooood."

Theia: "What's our first step, Daddy?" She blinked her big-ol' eyes at me.

K: "Does Opaline know more about the Umbrals?" I tilted my head to her.

Theia: "Yes. And now that I've grown, she lives in an old castle near here. It's on Mount Teratorn." She nodded.

K: "I remember where that is. That was Celestia's old winter vacation castle, before Luna returned." I shrugged.

Theia: "Yes. I let her use it." She shrugged. "Opaline is very knowledgeable. She taught me how to focus what little magic I have to do simple tricks." She looked out the window. "Like Mommy, I'm strongest at night, but I keep a schedule where I am awake in the mornings as well." She sighed. "I have to lead a country, after all."

Sunny: "I remember in the old legends, it said Celestia used to raise the sun-"

Theia: "OH! That old chestnut." She giggled. "Auntie Celestia didn't actually raise it - Equus revolves around the sun, as do the other planets. Did you think Equus was the center of the universe?" She smiled to Sunny.

Sunny: "....er, yeah, I kinda did." She blushed. "I feel silly."

K: "Don't." I nodded. "A lot of ponies thought that because of the ceremonies Celestia created to form a sort of religion around herself to keep ponykind focused on something other than the problems of racial integration two thousand years ago." I looked at Theia. "I'm guessing history only teaches the last few hundred years? And maybe some of the oldest legends?"

Theia: "We teach what we can. I excluded a lot of history from the curriculum. Mainly so ponies don't worry over things they cannot change." She shrugged her wings. "The history is still available if they go to the University." She smiled. "Most ponies, though, don't - you know how it is. You get your Mark and you do a job."

K: "Only now, instead of the Spell of Carefree being part of the reason, civilization being on a precipice is the driving factor." I frowned. "Celestia would've done the same thing, I think." And nodded. "For starters, we need to unite the Unity Crystal, though if Opaline is that close by, we may as well see her before we leave for Bridlewood." I nodded.

Theia: "You should know, Daddy." She looked down a moment. "Opaline.. is.. someone I allied with out of convenience. I do not fully trust her as I would Mommy or Auntie Celestia. I have long detected something from her that isn't honest." She frowned. "Other Daddy's powers gave me the ability to sense that."

K: "Dusk was one of the best Guards ever." I nodded. "Honestly, I thought so." I shrugged. "That you'd get something from him."

Sunny: "This is all so amazing to think about." She shook her head. "I want to know ALL the legends! And know the truth, not just the small stuff, and know where I might not have the full story."

Theia: "Of course, my Niece." She smiled.

So, now, I knew the situation. I knew I'd get no help from the past, it seemed. Theia didn't know the final tactical situation on the ground, and I knew she didn't even want to think about her brother and sisters, what might've become of them.. it hurt too much. But, we had a direction, and a plan.

Now? Ponykind needed to know that the Human of Legend had returned, and Pipp was the perfect pony to help me spread the message.

[End of Part 1]

Part 2: Declaration

TIME: 08:30 NERST
DATE: Redemption Day 10/16 Septimus, 2054 C.S.
LOCATION: Zephyr Heights, Greenbroke, New Equestria


Pipp set up her best camera - not the one on her phone - and had her lucky microphone and everything. All of it for what she Chirped out to the masses, would be a VERY SPECIAL announcement by Princess Theia, Prime Minister Haven, and a very special guest.

I was in my new Class "A" Formal Uniform. I deliberately designed it to resemble my old one. Black tunic, that came down to the mid thigh. Black slacks. Black boots. Grey shirt. Black tie. Black peaked cap.

I was a bit nervous.

Pipp: "You'll be fine, K! This. Is going to be SO fab." She slung her foreleg around me. "The Pippsqueaks and everyone in New Equestria is going to go cra-aaaa-zzaaeeeyy!" She sang that last word.

K: "I'm ready." I nodded.

Pipp: "Okay.. and.. here we go." She clicked it on. "HELLO PIPPSQUEAKS!" She paused, knowing her audience. "As you see, my mother, Prime Minister Haven, and Princess Theia are here. AND! We have a special guest!" She flourished a bow. "Now, let me give this over to the totally excellent, the wonderful, the Princess with the mostest.. Princess Theia!" She bowed again, and held the Mic for Theia.

Theia: "My Little Ponies. As you should all know from school, one thousand and fifty years ago, we fought a terrible war against Dark King Tirek. What most of you know, also, is that a mysterious entity from another place was there to lead us, teach us, and most.. most importantly.. love us like we were his own. That entity was my father. Those that know him, and know of him, know him as the Human of Legend, or to those in our valiant Armed Services, General K." She stood to one side. "I am.. most honored to announce to you all.. that he has returned." She raised a hoof and allowed me to step into frame.

I, too, waited. I knew there would be cheers, screaming, excitement, and more.

K: "MY.. Little Ponies. My daughter speaks the truth. One thousand years ago, I came to help you all." I lowered my head a bit, taking off my peaked cap. "It was.. my honor.. to do so. YOu are all so very, very special. You don't know HOW special. I am very sorry that I could not prevent what has happened. At the moment of my victory over Tirek, I was torn away from you all against my will, and sent back from whence I came." I looked to Theia. "I didn't even get to see my daughter grow up." And back to the camera. "You are all very, very special to me. And as I did once before, I pledge my life to you all. I would die at this moment if it meant undoing all the suffering that you've been through." I shook my head. "I could not stop it." And I nodded. "But, I will find a way to fix things. I promise. However, as it was before, so shall it be again - I cannot do ANY.. thing.. without your help." I gestured to the camera. "All of you, down to the youngest foal, together. United. Friends." I gently pulled Pipp closer. "All of us." I shook my head. "We must all stand together if we're to destroy the darkness that plagues our lands. And we CAN do it - I know how. Once more, I will give you all the tools you need. And, as I said, I don't care if I die in the process, so long as my life traded means you can ALL live in peace, harmony, and most especially, safety." I nodded to Pipp. "Pipp will provide regular updates as she can, to all her Pippsqueaks." I shrugged. "I guess I'm one, too, now." I smiled. "Together. We will overcome the Darkness once again." I got serious. "Military commanders can expect orders to come down within the next 24 hours."

Haven: "Civil leaders can expect Parliament to pass additional legislation for the new war effort very soon. But, this is not just war, this is our redemption, our salvation!" She stomped her hoof on the ground gently, but firmly. "We can unite. This kingdom is heir to a legacy - and we can be that legacy again!" She nodded. "Additional instructions to civil authorities will start to arrive within two to three days."

Theia: "Our kingdom has a reason, again, to be hopeful." She said, as Sunny, Hitch, Izzy, and Zipp joined us before the camera. "Answers will be coming soon. We will destroy the darkness, and live up to the beauty and splendor our nation had when I was a foal."

K: "Do not fear the darkness, any more, Little Ponies." I nodded to the camera. "For soon, WE shall be the bigger, scarier, monster in that darkness, bringing forth the light of our love and friendship to everyone. We will chase the darkness back into its hole, and we will defeat it once again." I placed my hand over my heart. "As I did one thousand years ago to the Immortal Sun Goddess Celestia, I swear now to ALL of you: My life, my everything, for you. For you all."

Pipp: "...and, cut, and clear." She started bouncing. "OH! That was FAA-BUU-LOOOUSS!" She hugged her mom. "You were great, Mom!"

Haven: "I certainly hope so!" She smiled.


TIME: 10:44 NERST


Haven and I were talking. She was VERY sweet. I really liked her. She had a very motherly vibe. I was telling her jokes from Earth, and funny memories I had from a thousand years ago. She was CACKLING at points. Zipp and Pipp were rolling. It was fun, seeing them kick up their little hooves.

Theia was just glad to spend some time with me, after so long.

Haven: "..had I known you were so charming as WELL as brave, I would have prayed for your return even harder." She smiled.

Zipp: "Well, Mom.. Galactica Spark provides. See?" She shrugged a bit.

K: "..yeah." I looked down at my snack.

I didn't want to tell them all the stress Galactica Spark put me through. Zipp seemed to worship her a lot. Moreso, so did Haven. Sunny and the others seemed neutral.

Religion was going to be touchy, here, I could see. Another minefield. No matter.

Theia: "I'm looking forward to spending time with you, Daddy." She smiled a bit. "It will take us time to work back up to a point where we can bring the nation to bear against this darkness." She nodded.

K: "Logistics win wars." I nodded. "First thing's first, we need to talk to Opaline, then unite the parts of the Unity Crystal."

Haven: "Yes." She nodded. "I know only of ours." She pointed her hoof to my pocket. "I don't know how it ended up in our family's possession, but.." She shook her head.

Sunny: "As we covered, I can imagine there's a piece in Bridlewood, given how it's mostly unicorns there, but.." She shook her head. "I have no idea what the Earth Crystal would look like, or where it might be."

Haven: "Sunny, dear, I'm sure we can find it." She smiled to her. "Oh, you kids are so delightful." She smiled. "I feel like a filly again."

K: "I'm glad, Haven." I gave her a smile back. "One thing the war taught me, is, even in the face of tragedy, we can find happiness and joy. That's one thing you little ponies have - you are absolutely able to smile despite the worst rain." I nodded a bit to Haven. "You said you wanted a son like me - I wish my mom had been as supportive of me as you've been."

Theia: "I'll arrange transportation to Opaline's castle." She nodded.

Hitch: "I can drive!" He smiled, having swallowed his sandwich quickly to speak up.

Zipp: "Cool." She smiled at Hitch. "I mean, not everyone can." She lilted her head to the side.

This time, I did see Sunny give a glare to Zipp. A defensive one, like she wanted to step in, but she didn't quite feel like she should.

I'd talk to her later.

Pipp: "Ohhh! I can't wait to make some MYUUU-HUUU-ZIIIC....!" She sang that last word. "Oh, my gosh. I'm going to-" She looked at me. "-you think everypony'll want me to?"

K: "You mean, inspire them." I said, as she was so animated, she was almost skipping the point.

Pipp nodded, and beamed a grin.

Izzy: "Oh, I'm suuuure everypony will just sparkle and shine when they hear you, Pipp." She leaned over and hooked Pipp's neck in her foreleg, waving wide.

K: "Happiness is where you find it." He nodded. "Pipp, I think you can keep everypony happy - you have a special talent for music, and music has a magic all its own."

Theia: "Yes... yes, it is!" She raised her head. "I think.. Daddy.. we've been so focused on the magic we've lost all this time, we've forgotten the magic that's left!" She clopped her hoof on the table.

K: "There IS other kinds of magic. I know Sigil magic still works, and that's based on spiritual power, not mana." I waved a hand. "I... tried to use a Wind Sigil. And it nearly knocked me out." I shrugged.

Theia: "Yes.." She blinked. "..again,-"

K: "Opaline. She would know." I nodded again. "How long will it take to get there by car?"

Theia: "An hour, maybe less." She shrugged. "Total travel time would be about two." She nodded, and smiled.

K: "I'd like to go as soon ss possible." I said, hugging Theia again. "Not that I don't want to spend time with my little girl, all grown up now."

Theia: "Of course." She nodded. "Sergeant Zoom." She motioned for one of the Royal Guards to come over.

1st SGT Zoom Zephyrwing: "Yes, Majesty." He bowed slightly to her. "I'll get a vehicle prepared."

I eyed him up. I could see dedication, but I wanted to know what else was going on upstairs. I needed a new CAG, and that meant a lot of ponies all specialized in different things.

K: "We'll go in the morning. I'd like to spend the day doing interviews. I'm sure Pipp would love to." I smiled to her.

Pipp: "YES!" She cheered. "Think you'd like to do another song with me?!"

K: "My honor." I bowed my head.

I had no idea how fateful my interaction with Opaline would be.

Part 3: Mist Opal

TIME: 07:30 NERST
DATE: Redemption Day 11/17 Septimus, 2054 C.S.
LOCATION: Opaline's Castle, Greenbroke, New Equestria


Celestia's old vacation castle. I had seen it before, during the war. It was smaller, cozier, and more like a "royal home" than a palace. We drove up to the outside, and I smiled. It hung off Mount Teratorn like a miniature Canterlot Castle. It gave me a sense of familiarity.

We got out of the Tactical Vehicle, and as we did, I could feel like I was being watched. I wasn't a magic adept. But, I remembered how things felt. I remembered how magic flowed. And with so little of it around, it was easier to feel what there was.

I walked up to the door, and simply knocked.

What answered was a very timid, blue-coated pony with fading dark-to-red hair, and a braid along the left side of her head. I was immediately reminded of Fluttershy. She looked up at me, with a timid blush.

Misty: "I'm.. Misty. Uh." She looked at the distant horizon. "Bright.. dawn." She blinked. "Yeah, Misty Brightdawn."

I cocked a brow at her.

Opaline: "Miiistyyy?" She chimed from inside the castle. "Who's at the door? If it's those Luna's Witnesses again, just use the garden hose, like last time."

I laughed. Hard. It was all too hilarious.

K: "No, no, I'm General K!" I called into the castle. "I'm here to see you, Opaline!"

There was a pause, a lot of shuffling of things, and a crash, then Opaline opened the door fully, with very obviously freshly brushed hair, and a headdress hastily applied.

Opaline: "Well!" She attempted an air of superiority. "The Human of Legend. As I expected, exactly when I expected."

K: "..uh-huh. Bullshit." I nodded. "One of your hoof-shoes is a slipper."

She looked at her right back hoof, realized a bunny slipper was on there instead of her fetlock greave, and quickly hid it behind her back.

Opaline: "Ah-hah-ha!" She bowed her head. "How silly!" She beckoned us in. "Come in, come in."

I entered, followed by my team.

K: "I can see the Alicorn air of superiority about you." I smiled, as everyone came in. "Misty." I nodded to her.

Misty: "Ah.. hi." She lowered her head.

Introverted. Very introverted.

Opaline: "Misty? Please get our guests some tea, hmm?" She was polite, but direct - a teacher's attitude, for sure. "I wish to speak to the human.. alloooone.." She drawled that last part out.

Misty: "Ah.. yes.. Opaline." She led the others to the kitchen area.

I followed Opaline to the upstairs area - she had arcane arts things all over the place. I recognized a few of them from different places. Wortnester's Serum! I thought of Cody Allcome.

K: "She's.." I looked back towards the stairs.

Opaline: "Her father brought her to me." She shrugged. "I took her in. Once I became aware of the Umbrals, and.. uhm.. the sacrifice of my.." She blinked. "..they were my cousins, K.." She looked down. "..I knew I was.. expected to help. Somehow." She shrugged her wings. "Twilight.. and Sunset.. they did so well." She sighed. "But, you should know, the Umbrals are going to come back. And soon." She nodded firmly to me.

K: "Misty.. is.. Alphabittle's daughter." I nodded.

Opaline: "Yes, she isss.." She flourished very slightly. "Misty is very, very special."

K: "So Alphabittle told me." I nodded.

Opaline: "I've taught Misty the magic needed to fight them directly. She had potential, K. When I saw her, I knew immediately, she had the potential to use this magic. It's.. it's not easy to cast, you know?" She nodded slowly.

K: "Really? So, is she, like The Chosen One, or something?" I tilted my head.

Opaline: "Ahhh.. not quite.. more like.. she's our last real hope to resist these things. Not every Unicorn has the proper alignments to cast these spells effectively. She's an incredibly rare Triple Alignment. Do you know how our magic works?" She nodded to me.

K: "Somewhat." I nodded back.

Opaline: "It's been sealed over the last thousand years, but, in short, she's a Solar-Void-Light." She sighed a bit.

K: "I see. No wonder you whisked her away so fast." I nodded. "Let me guess - the Umbrals would've become aware of her, and she would've put everyone in danger."

Opaline: "Yes." She turned to me. "This tower has a great deal of magical wards and protections. Sigil magic." She raised a hoof - and swapped the bunny slipper for her proper greave. "I've taught Misty much of it. Everything I can in fifteen years. Potions and Zebronic alchemy, everything I can to make up for the lack of proper magic."

K: "That must've been a lot." I said, pacing around the room, looking at everything, pretending to be disinterested in everything. "Fifteen years. On top of a thousand plotting to take the throne for yourself?" I slowly turned to her. "I'm old - not dumb."

Opaline: "EH?!" She was taken aback.

K: "Theia has the innate ability to sense trust and honesty. She told me she felt something dishonest from you. Given the breadth and depth of your knowledge, and your clear air of superiority, you feel as I do - that an Alicorn makes the best leader of the Little Ponies, at least from a Guardian perspective." I leaned over her table at her, and slid a hand over to one of her vials. "Wortnester's Serum. Useful for identifying anything, if you know what colors it changes, how it smells, and so on." I raised back a bit. "Be honest with me, Opaline. Right now, we're equals."

Opaline: "...." She looked down. "Rrrgh!" She raised her head and glared at me. "Theia was right - you ARE smart." She snorted.. then tossed her head like an angry horse. "You're right - I do think I.. that an Alicorn.. Should lead." She paced over to me, into my personal space. "But you.. created issue." And blinked, looking right into my eyes. "Princess Theia is heir to the throne, rightfully. I prefer to be.." She tilted her head. "..Eminence Grise." She trilled that out.

I leaned all the way in to lock eyes with her.

K: "You'd be a villain if the Umbrals weren't here." I nodded. "Nice." I shrugged. "And so.. you decided that the only thing to do was to save the world, and prove Alicorn superiority." I shrugged. "Thing is.." I booped her snootle. "..I know you better than you know yourself."

Opaline: "..what?" She tilted her head, reacting to the snootle boop, looking down at the tip of her own nose.

K: "You're called to heroism. Your real, true desire is to protect all of Ponykind. YOU just want to be adored as the hero you see yourself as. YOU want to be worshipped. YOU want ponies to recognize you as the powerful, wise, respectable Alicorn you see yourself to be. You have an ego that needs fed." I was reminded of Rainbow Dash. "But, you're also beholden to etiquette, honor, and tradition. You refuse to break it, as it's so important for you to be respected. So, you can never directly act against Theia, and moreover, you need to outshine her and you NEED to have the ponies of the world actively CHOOSE to elevate you to Queenhood." I smirked. "Just like a Mobster. You need that respect, or your victories would be as hollow as your heart."

Opaline: "M-My heart!?" She stepped back, NOW just realizing she was socially outmaneuvered.

K: "..you need love." I said, softly. "All Ponies do." I leaned over and - taking my lessons from Twilight herself - I hugged her around the neck.

She froze.

She'd never felt this before.

She'd never been accepted before.

She'd never once felt like someone cared about her, genuinely cared about her.

But, she could feel.. that I really, really did care about her, about her fate, and about her as a pony.

She froze.. and then leaned in, slumped in to me.

We slid down to the floor, and I just held her. After a minute, she started crying.

I called it perfectly - my instincts were coming back to me. Even if my body wasn't as powerful as it once was, my MIND was as sharp as it once was. I realized, looking into her and her motivations and actions, that as much as she wanted to see herself as superior to everypony, she was missing the most important thing in any pony's life.

Love.

She just wanted someone to love and care about her. It was part of the reason she absconded with Misty. It was part of the reason, I was sure, she'd been plotting devious acts for the last thousand years - she wanted to be adored by the masses, to replace the love she desperately desired.

She just didn't know how else to express it.

And with one act of genuine kindness, I broke her. I broke her resolve, already weakened by her need to protect and guard ponykind. She'd already let a little bit of concern and worry FOR OTHERS creep into her heart. I just finished it off.


TIME: 09:27 NERST


We came downstairs, and Opaline's eyeshadow was gone. She'd spent time crying, and we spent time talking. She confessed that ever since she decided SHE needed to be THE Guardian of Ponykind, she realized that she was all alone. That her heart hurt.

I admit, that if she hadn't started down this path, her heart would likely would have remained locked off from my kindness. She WOULD have been a Villain.. but the Umbrals made the situation different.
