There was thundering applause and cheering. Dignitaries from all over NEIGHTO were here. Citizens from every NEIGHTO nation. Honor guards. Soldiers. Sailors. Airponies.

I could feel their emotions running higher than the clouds themselves, with determination, with reverence, and with pride.

She was tearing up, but her face remained stoic, as I affixed her new rank to her, and wound a red sash around her hip, secured with a General Officer's sword belt, and a Marine Sabre.

I saluted her, as did we all. As with the others, her promotion was cheered.

Ochre was floored.. Sigmask had to help him to the podium. Sigmask knew what was coming - Ochre didn't.

K: "Master Gunnery Sergeant Sigmask, you've served in the Marines since the first time they were used in anger, and demonstrated singular skill at eliminating the enemy, and in leadership of your fellow ponies. "Ochre Wave. YOU were once branded a coward! YOU were once SHAMED before all of Equestria. And what was your response? To discard your former station as a Navy Officer, and enlist in the Marines, knowing well that you would see combat almost immediately. And in that combat, you proved yourself not only capable, and skilled, but brave. Whatever shame darkened your past.. whatever misdeeds you may have done.. by the power vested in me by our Eternal Princesses of Sun and Moon, I hereby grant you full and complete absolution. May those misdeeds never ride the lips of another pony for all time - and may you be only remembered as a brave, honorable, skilled leader of troops. As I pinned on his new rank, I could almost feel him shaking. I couldn't blame him - I'd basically wiped his past entirely clean, erasing the Court Martial, as though it never happened. He was no longer a Marine "with a past." He was a Gunnery Sergeant, a leader of Ponies.

Sigmask had to help him back to his seat, he was so moved.

And as I have said many times, I would gladly throw all of that away if it meant none of you would suffer, and I alone would face the dangers and bear the risk. Two years ago today, that danger was brought to the forefront. In the last two years, we have all lost something so dear to us. For some, it was family. For some, it was their home. For some, it was everything. But, we will not falter, we will not fail, and we will not wilt in the face of avarice. We will prevail." I bowed to all of them respectfully.

The ceremony went on like this for another half hour - thankfully, unlike human officials, ponies don't make a point of long speeches.

It was the dance afterwards that I was looking forwards to. Just me, and.. them. Applejack, Luna, Rarity, and Dusty.. I danced with all of them. Unlike the Grand Galloping Gala, the dances were short, and there were only four of them. Brief, in keeping with the reverence for the day.

Rarity: "..yes, Marelin was fun.." She wiggled her eyebrows back.

Part 5: Iberian Pop

TIME: 08:26 Iberian Time/09:26 Greenwich Mean Time
DATE: War Day 732/6 Quartus 1004 C.S.
LOCATION: Inside a Dungeon, Girul, Navarre

Applejack: "WON'T SEE US AS INVADERS, HUH!?" She was still sore with me.

Starlight: "Look, AJ, I'm sure once someone official comes down to talk to us, we can clear this all up." She sighed.

K: "We should be glad they didn't shoot us on sight." I frowned. "I'd love to know how they got firearms like that."

Indeed, once we got to the village of Girul, the local constabulary had simply ambushed us at the edge of town, armed with assault rifles and an emplaced machine gun.. and taken us prisoner. There was little talking, little of anything, other than ordering us to "Vamos!" into this dungeon.

Applejack hadn't even slept next to me that night - Dusty did, though. AJ was very, very sore at me, over this.

Rarity and Starlight talked about the situation most of the night, and Starlight was convinced she could talk her way out of this - she could speak Navarre (which is basically what I'd call Spanish). As could I. Living in California did have its perks. Gorcat? My loyal pal, he'd made sure to stay between me and their bayonets, his muscles constantly coiled and ready to strike, if I gave the order.

Firecracker grumbled about the situation, but didn't outright complain or call me out. She had slept as close to Gorcat as she could get, though, clearly showing she had no issue with Griffons.

Gorcat: "Come on, now, Jackie. It's too early to start in like that." He grunted. "K apologized. A lot."

Applejack: "Ah still say this were a boneheaded mistake." She frowned.

K: "Oh, I agree. I had no idea they'd do THIS. I thought they'd challenge us at the border, not let us get all the way to town, and then ambush us." I shrugged. "Moreover, I had no idea they'd already designed and built their own indigenous firearms. I thought that'd be something we'd have to teach them." I shook my head, slowly.

Suddenly, outside, I could hear commotion.. then shooting.. some screaming, yelling.. more shooting.. dying. And then.. silence.

Gorcat: "Someone just had a fight, out there. I wonder who won?" He crossed his arms.

K: "Hopefully, someone that likes us!" I grinned, chipperly.

Rarity shot me a look.

After a few minutes, I could hear hooves clomping down the stairs from the first floor, and around the corner, came Ibexian soldiers in much, much different uniforms.. including someone very important in a rather formal uniform and bicorne hat sitting regally between his goat horns.

Duke Condor: "Buen dia, mi amigos!" He slid his soldiers aside, and moved to the center of the antechamber outside of the cells, so we could see him clearly. "Soy el Duque Condor." He bowed to us, each, in turn. "Serias el humano?" He looked at me.

K: "Si, Duque." I bowed to him. "Mariscal-de-campo, del Ejercito Real de Equestria, K---- K---."

Duke Condor: "Excelente! Senor Mariscal.." He looked to my party.

K: "Do you speak Equestrian?" I asked, my limited Spanish running low on adjectives..

Duke Condor: "Si, Senor Mariscal. I do." He said in a very, very refined Navarrian (Spanish) accent.

K: "This is my wife, Applejack." I gestured to her. "Rarity Belle, Firecracker Burst, Gorcat, Starlight, and Dusty Dunes." To each, in turn.

Duke Condor: "Ahh.." He nodded with a big grin, then grew serious, but still, not hostile. "Why do you come?" He tilted his head, swishing his goat horns a bit.

K: "I was hoping to establish diplomatic realtions with Navarre and Lusitania." I nodded slightly to Starlight. "She's an Ambassador of Equestria. Our mission is peaceful."

Duke Condor: *HAHA!* "YOU..?" He seemed jovial at the concept. "..with US!?" He looked at his soldiers. "Han venido buscando alianza con Navarra!"

They all laughed, like I'd just told the funniest joke they'd ever heard in their lives. One even fell over, holding his belly. Truly, this world's purity was not reserved for Ponies exclusively.

Duke Condor: "Senor Mariscal!" He paused, shaking his head. "Our nation is in Civil War!!" He waved his forelegs out, and shook his head. "There IS no single government of Navarre!"

Everyone else laughed a bit more, then the laughter died down, again.

Duke Condor: "Now.. Senors, Senora, and Senoritas.. I think, maybe, you would like to get out of here, yes?" He tilted his head, and nodded back to one of his troops. "Come, I will explain."

One of his soldiers fetched the keys off the wall, and unlocked our dungeon cage. We hadn't been shackled, so, this was fine. I exited first, and extended my hand to Duke Condor.

K: "A pleasure to meet you, sire." I said, addressing him formally.

He looked at my hand a second - having never seen one before, of course - and then put his hoof in it, giving it a grip and a shake.

Duke Condor: "The pleasure is mine, Senor Mariscal. Your Navarese is.. decent." He gave me a small smirk.

K: "Gracias, Duque." I nodded sharply to him. "I learned what - on my Earth - is called Mexican Spanish, to a degree. You'd call it Mulexican Navarese." I shrugged slightly. "It's far more informal in tone and inflection than formal Spanish - Navarese, that is."

Duke Condor: "I had heard you speak'a languages!" He chortled to himself. "This Civil War does not happen in a total vacuum. Come, come, the Herdiets will be back soon enough. We must escape."

Applejack: "Hey, any chance'a us gettin' our stuff back? We'd really 'preciate it, Yer Highness." She said, getting right up next to me - angry or not, she was still my wife.

Duke Condor: "Si, si. It is upstairs. Come, come." He gestured.

We mounted the stairs and found he had another squad of Navarran Ibex troops up here. The uniforms reminded me of the Spanish Guarda Civil, and I surmised that, if this was somehow analogous to the Spanish Civil War (and it certainly seemed as much), they likely WERE Guarda Civil, as almost half the force defected to the Nationalists, of which Royalists were a part.

We gathered our equipment and weapons, but when I grabbed my rifle, I made a show of removing the magazine and racking the bolt to clear the chamber. Both to reassure the Duke that we had peaceful intentions, and to show his troops we meant no harm - I was sure a few of them were rather testy and on-edge about Ponies, of all things, having weapons. Everyone copied me.

K: "Which way?" I nodded to him.

Duke Condor: "Come, this way."

He took the lead of his soldiers, and led the way out of the police building, then led us south, out of town, and we marched in silence for about ten or fifteen minutes, until more of his troops caught up with us, with literal horse-drawn one-pony cargo carts, loaded with supplies. I could tell they'd basically looted the town - there was food, ammunition, weapons, and sundry other useful things. They took no tools or other things one might use to manufacture items, which told me they were looting for an active army that had no immediate need of such things.

TIME: 10:11 IT
LOCATION: Hidden Mountain Redoubt, 7km south of Girul

We arrived at his camp at about ten past ten, and he quickly ushured us inside. After a bit more walking, we came to an Estate house of some kind. It was clear this mountain redoubt was once an active ranch of some sort, and the Estate house belonged to a local Don, or the Alcalde of the region.

We entered the main lobby, which was VERY Spanish in construction and color scheme, and were greeted by a lady about the Count's age, bearing a platter of cheese, wine, and juice.

Duquesa Condor: "Bien dia! Soy la Duquesa Condor.." She nodded to her husband, and they nuzzled when he approached her. "..Bienvenidas mis invitadas."

K: "Gracias, Duquesa. Soy el Mariscal K--- del Equestria Ejercito Real." I placed my hand to my chest, and bowed to her slightly.

I noted that Duke Condor's coat was dark with streaks of grey, whereas hers was a 'technicolor' dark-with-tan. If I remembered my Ibexes right, this defined both of them as Northern Spanish Ibex.

Then, it all came together - I was talking to the Equus equivalent of Duke Conde, leader of the Carlists faction of the Nationalist Front of the Spanish Civil War. While I gave this world credit for having generally nicer, kinder people, I also remembered that Duke Conde was.. not someone you wanted to cross.

Duquesa Condor: "Por qu‚ no dijiste que ¡bamos a tener invitados?" She asked her husband. I caught.. part of that.

Duke Condor: "Equestrian, please, my dear. They.. do not ALL speak Navarese." He smiled.

Starlight: "Si, Duquesa." She bowed slightly to her. "Mi nombre es Starlight. Embajadora de Su Alteza la Princesa Celestia."

Duquesa Condor: "Ah! Your Navarese is.. interesting." She looked at her a bit. "You sound like my great-grandmother.."

Starlight: "I am from eight thousand years in the past.." She shrugged. "I was rescued by Marshal K." She said, using the transliteration of my rank title.

Duquesa Condor: "Ah! How exciting!" She beamed a grin to her husband. "So, does this mean we will now have help?"

Duke Condor: "Ehh.. we rescued them from the Herdiets.. I do not yet know their intentions, but they mean us no harm, at the least. Please, they must be hungry, mi amore." He nodded to her, and she nodded back.

Duquesa Condor: "I will see you have something proper to eat. Uno momento, por favor." She scooted towards the back of the house, and started commanding Kitchen Staff.

K: "You're a team." I nodded to the Duke.

Duke Condor: "Indeed we are.." He stared at her flanks as she sashayed away. "..and she only grows more beautiful with each passing day."

K: "I know the feeling." I put my arm around Applejack's neck.. she didn't resist, and she did look up at me with those bedroom eyes - I had a feeling, seeing the Duke and Duchess's mutual affection, reminded her.

Duke Condor: "Now, Mariscal K.." He looked at Starlight, then to me. "..I should, I think, describe our situation.. and I think, perhaps, you may understand.. we need your help. It will interest you to know.. just how Tirek is involved." He turned to me, with a very determined curl to his lips.
