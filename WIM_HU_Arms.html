Herdiet Union: Firearms



Herdiet doctrine is very similar to Soviet doctrine fron Earth, save that their society thrives on gradual conquest, and brutality within the Regime is far, far more excessive. Weapons technology, until K the Human arrived on Equus, was focused on hydraulics and springs, and a means to project bolts of various size further distances, with greater precision. When they discovered firearms, they already had the technical knowledge of advanced weaponcraft, and set about making their own designs based on K's principles and basic inventions. Unlike Equestria, the Herdiets HAD been successful in refining the Eastern Bloc pistol powder formula into something more suitable for rifles, and immediately took off with it. By the time K was captured by the Herdiets almost exactly one year after the Attack on Canterlot, and over two years since the Sherman started low rate initial production, the Herdiets had firearms for every occasion, including true Heavy Machine Guns, and even Machine Pistols - something K had not developed at all! As a result of espionage, however, Herdiet weapons designs were leaked to the Collective Khyangese Republic, and these weapons were copied with some basic alterations.


KDP-71 Heavy Machine Gun
WEAPON TYPE: Heavy Machine Gun, Wheeled Mount
CALIBER: 7.92x52mm
CAPACITY: Non-Disintegrating Metal Link Belt, various size ammo cans
EFFECTIVE RANGE: 400 meters (point target),
800 meters (area target)
BARREL LENGTH: 440mm/17.32 inches
OVERALL LENGTH: 1 meter/3.28 feet
OVERALL HEIGHT: 466mm/18.34 inches
TOTAL MASS: 37lbs/16.8kg
The KDP-71 coincidentally resembles the Maxim Machine Gun used by the Soviet Union in World War II, right down to the small wheeled mount. This heavy monster is, of course, too large to carry. The huge water jacket allows the weapon to sustain its 540-round-per-minute rate of fire indefinitely, as long as the water holds out. The 7.92x52mm round has incredibly good range, and good precision. The Herdiets discovered that if the barrel rifling in the KDP-71 was cut shallow, and "softened" at the tip of the barrel, they could achieve a wide fire dispersal without having to slide the weapon back and forth, making it far easier to use as an area denial weapon. Indeed, it shoots about 6-8 MOA, compared to an Equestrian Bradley, that shoots about 3.
